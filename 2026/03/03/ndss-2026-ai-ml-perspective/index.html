NDSS 2026 论文精选 - AI 与机器学习安全视角

从 AI/ML 视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖 LLM 安全、机器学习攻防、AI 驱动的安全分析

Conference: NDSS 2026 | Perspective: AI & Machine Learning Security
Total Papers in this perspective: 25+ papers

一、大型语言模型 (LLM) 安全

1. A Causal Perspective for Enhancing Jailbreak Attack and Defense

Authors: Licheng Pan (Zhejiang University), Yunsheng Lu (University of Chicago), Jiexi Liu (Alibaba Group), et al.

English: Applies causal analysis to LLM jailbreak attacks and defenses, identifying causal relationships between prompts and outputs to improve both attack effectiveness and defense mechanisms.

中文: 将因果分析应用于 LLM 越狱攻击和防御，识别提示和输出之间的因果关系以提高攻击有效性和防御机制。

2. ACE: A Security Architecture for LLM-Integrated App Systems

Authors: Evan Li et al. (Northeastern University)

English: Proposes comprehensive security architecture for LLM-integrated applications, addressing data leakage, prompt injection, and API access control challenges.

中文: 为 LLM 集成应用提出全面的安全架构，解决数据泄露、提示注入和 API 访问控制挑战。

3. Attention is All You Need to Defend Against Indirect Prompt Injection

Authors: Yinan Zhong et al. (Zhejiang University)

English: Demonstrates that attention mechanisms alone can effectively defend against indirect prompt injection without additional overhead.

中文: 证明仅使用注意力机制就可以有效防御间接提示注入，无需额外开销。

4. Beyond Jailbreak: Unveiling Risks from Blurred Capability Boundaries

Authors: Yunyi Zhang et al. (Tsinghua University)

English: Identifies security risks arising from unclear boundaries between different LLM capabilities, leading to privilege escalation and information leakage.

中文: 识别因不同 LLM 能力之间界限模糊而产生的安全风险，导致权限提升和信息泄露。

5. Bleeding Pathways: Vanishing Discriminability in LLM Hidden States

Authors: Yingjie Zhang et al. (IIE, CAS; Ant Group)

English: Reveals how vanishing discriminability in LLM hidden states enables jailbreak attacks, providing theoretical analysis and practical demonstrations.

中文: 揭示 LLM 隐藏状态中消失的可辨别性如何启用越狱攻击，提供理论分析和实际演示。

6. Cache Me, Catch You: Cache Threats in LLM Serving Frameworks

Authors: XiangFan Wu (Ocean University of China; QI-ANXIN)

English: Uncovers cache-related vulnerabilities in LLM serving frameworks that leak sensitive information across user sessions.

中文: 揭示 LLM 服务框架中与缓存相关的漏洞，可在用户会话之间泄露敏感信息。

7. Character-Level Perturbations Disrupt LLM Watermarks

Authors: Zhaoxi Zhang et al. (UTS, Griffith University)

English: Shows character-level perturbations can remove watermarks from LLM-generated text, challenging content attribution systems.

中文: 展示字符级扰动可以从 LLM 生成的文本中移除水印，挑战内容归属系统。

8. Characterizing Censorship in Chinese LLM Services

Authors: Anna Ablove et al. (University of Michigan, UC Berkeley)

English: Empirical study of censorship implementation in Chinese LLM services, revealing filtering techniques and effectiveness.

中文: 对中国 LLM 服务中审查实施的实证研究，揭示过滤技术和有效性。

9. Chasing Shadows: Pitfalls in LLM Security Research

Authors: Jonathan Evertz et al. (CISPA, Max Planck, et al.)

English: Meta-analysis identifying common methodological pitfalls in LLM security research, providing guidance for rigorous evaluation.

中文: 元分析识别 LLM 安全研究中常见的方法论陷阱，为严格评估提供指导。

10. DUALBREACH: Efficient Dual-Jailbreaking via Multi-Target Optimization

Authors: Xinzhe Huang et al. (Zhejiang University)

English: Efficient dual-jailbreaking approach attacking multiple LLM safety mechanisms simultaneously through multi-target optimization.

中文: 通过多目标优化同时攻击多个 LLM 安全机制的高效双重越狱方法。

二、机器学习模型安全

11. A Hard-Label Black-Box Evasion Attack Against ML Traffic Detection

Authors: Zixuan Liu et al. (Tsinghua University, Beijing Institute of Technology)

English: Hard-label black-box attack fooling ML-based malicious traffic detection with minimal queries and no model knowledge.

中文: 用最少查询和不了解模型的硬标签黑盒攻击欺骗基于 ML 的恶意流量检测。

12. Achieving Zen: Mathematical and Programmatic Model Representations

Authors: David Oygenblik et al. (Georgia Tech)

English: Combines mathematical and programmatic deep learning model representations for better attribution and reuse detection, addressing IP concerns.

中文: 结合数学和程序化深度学习模型表示以更好地归属和检测重用，解决知识产权问题。

13. Unified Defense Against Membership Inference in Federated Learning

Authors: Liwei Zhang et al. (Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications)

English: Unified framework combining distillation and contribution-aware aggregation to defend against membership inference in FL.

中文: 结合蒸馏和贡献感知聚合的统一框架，防御联邦学习中的成员推理攻击。

14. Beyond Raw Bytes: Towards Large Malware Language Models

Authors: Luke Kurlandski et al. (Rochester Institute of Technology)

English: Explores LLMs specifically trained on malware data, moving beyond raw byte analysis to understand malicious code semantics.

中文: 探索专门在恶意软件数据上训练的 LLM，超越原始字节分析以理解恶意代码语义。

15. Cascading and Proxy Membership Inference Attacks

Authors: Yuntao Du et al. (Purdue University)

English: Cascading and proxy membership inference attacks improving success rates while requiring less target information.

中文: 级联和代理成员推理攻击提高成功率，同时需要更少的目标信息。

16. CatBack: Universal Backdoor Attacks on Tabular Data

Authors: Behrad Tajalli et al. (Radboud University, Delft University)

English: Universal backdoor attacks on tabular data via categorical encoding manipulation, affecting financial/healthcare ML models.

中文: 通过分类编码操纵对表格数据的通用后门攻击，影响金融/医疗 ML 模型。

17. Causal-Guided Detoxify Backdoor Attack of Open-Weight LoRA Models

Authors: Linzhi Chen et al. (ShanghaiTech University)

English: Causal-guided approach to inject and detoxify backdoors in open-weight LoRA models, revealing parameter-efficient fine-tuning vulnerabilities.

中文: 因果引导方法在开放权重 LoRA 模型中注入和解毒后门，揭示参数高效微调漏洞。

18. Constructive Noise Defeats Adversarial Noise

Authors: Meng Shen et al. (Beijing Institute of Technology)

English: Uses constructive noise to defeat adversarial noise, providing effective adversarial example detection for commercial DNN services.

中文: 使用建设性噪声对抗对抗性噪声，为商业 DNN 服务提供有效的对抗样本检测。

19. Dataset Reduction and Watermark Removal via Self-supervised Learning

Authors: Hao Luan et al. (Fudan University, Worcester Polytechnic Institute)

English: Demonstrates using self-supervised learning to reduce dataset requirements and remove watermarks in model extraction attacks.

中文: 展示使用自监督学习减少数据集需求并在模型提取攻击中移除水印。

20. DNN Latency Sequencing: Extracting Architectures from SGX Enclaves

Authors: Minkyung Park et al. (UT Dallas, Purdue University)

English: Extracts DNN architectures from Intel SGX enclaves using single-stepping attacks based on latency sequencing patterns.

中文: 使用基于延迟序列模式的单步攻击从 Intel SGX 安全区提取 DNN 架构。

三、AI 驱动的安全分析

21. An LLM-Driven Fuzzing Framework for Detecting PLC Logic Bugs

Authors: Jiaxing Cheng et al. (IIE CAS, NJUST, Virginia Tech)

English: Leverages LLMs to drive PLC fuzzing, detecting critical logic instruction bugs in industrial control systems.

中文: 利用 LLM 驱动 PLC 模糊测试，检测工业控制系统中的关键逻辑指令错误。

22. FirmAgent: LLM Agents for IoT Firmware Vulnerability Discovery

Authors: Jiangan Ji et al. (Information Engineering University, Tsinghua)

English: Combines fuzzing with LLM agents to enhance IoT firmware vulnerability discovery through intelligent analysis.

中文: 结合模糊测试与 LLM 代理通过智能分析增强物联网固件漏洞发现。

23. CHAMELEOSCAN: LLM-Powered iOS Chameleon App Detection

Authors: Hongyu Lin et al. (Zhejiang University)

English: Uses LLM-powered UI exploration to detect iOS apps that change behavior after App Store review.

中文: 使用 LLM 驱动的 UI 探索检测在 App Store 审核后改变行为的 iOS 应用。

24. Chimera: Multi-Agent LLMs for Automatic Insider Threat Simulation

Authors: Jiongchi Yu et al. (Singapore Management University, Tianjin University)

English: Harnesses multi-agent LLMs to automatically simulate insider threats, improving security testing and training.

中文: 利用多智能体 LLM 自动模拟内部威胁，改进安全测试和培训。

25. Decompiling the Synergy: Human-LLM Teaming in Reverse Engineering

Authors: Zion Basque et al. (Arizona State University, University of Padua)

English: Empirical study of human-LLM collaboration in software reverse engineering, revealing synergies and challenges.

中文: 人类-LLM 协作在软件逆向工程中的实证研究，揭示协同效应和挑战。

研究趋势与洞察

🔥 热点趋势

  1. LLM 安全成为主流: 从越狱攻击到防御机制，LLM 安全研究占据重要位置
  2. AI 赋能安全分析: LLM 被广泛用于漏洞发现、固件分析、逆向工程
  3. 模型窃取与保护: 模型归属、水印、知识产权保护成为关键议题
  4. 联邦学习隐私: 成员推理、贡献感知等 FL 特有安全问题
  5. 跨模态攻击: 从文本到代码、从图像到表格数据的攻击多样化

💡 关键洞察

  • 防御机制创新: 从架构设计(如 ACE)到注意力机制的原生防御
  • 攻击技术进化: 黑盒攻击更高效、后门攻击更隐蔽、越狱攻击更系统化
  • 可解释性需求: 研究开始关注方法论的科学性和可重现性
  • 实用化趋势: 从学术原型到可部署系统的转变

🌍 实际应用

  • 企业 AI 安全: LLM 集成应用的安全架构设计
  • 模型版权保护: 模型归属检测、水印技术
  • 隐私保护 ML: 联邦学习、差分隐私
  • AI 辅助安全: 固件分析、漏洞发现、恶意软件检测
