NDSS 2026 论文精选 - 网络与基础设施视角

从网络/基础设施视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖互联网路由、DNS、Web 安全、电子邮件协议

Perspective: Network & Infrastructure Security | Focus: Routing, DNS, Web, Email, Wi-Fi

一、互联网路由安全 (RPKI)

1. Crack in the Armor: Infrastructure Threats to RPKI Publication Points

Authors: Yunhao Liu et al. (Tsinghua University, Fudan University)

English: Identifies underlying infrastructure threats to RPKI publication point reachability that could undermine internet routing security.

中文: 识别对 RPKI 发布点可达性的底层基础设施威胁，可能破坏互联网路由安全。

2. Demystifying RPKI-Invalid Prefixes: Hidden Causes and Risks

Authors: Weitong Li et al. (Virginia Tech, CableLabs)

English: Uncovers hidden causes of RPKI-invalid prefixes and analyzes their security risks in global routing systems.

中文: 揭示 RPKI 无效前缀的隐藏原因并分析其在全球路由系统中的安全风险。

二、DNS 与缓存攻击

3. Continuous User Behavior Monitoring via DNS Cache Timing Attacks

Authors: Hannes Weissteiner et al. (Graz University of Technology, University of Vienna)

English: Demonstrates continuous user behavior monitoring through DNS cache timing attacks without network access or malware.

中文: 展示通过 DNS 缓存时序攻击进行持续用户行为监控，无需网络访问或恶意软件。

三、Web 应用安全

4. Achieving Interpretable DL-based Web Attack Detection

Authors: Peiyang Li et al. (Tsinghua University, Ant Group, et al.)

English: Achieves interpretable deep learning-based web attack detection by localizing malicious payloads for analyst verification.

中文: 通过定位恶意载荷实现可解释的基于深度学习的 Web 攻击检测，供分析师验证。

5. DOM-XSS Detection via Webpage Interaction Fuzzing

Authors: Nuno Sabino et al. (CMU, IST Lisbon, et al.)

English: Detects DOM-based XSS vulnerabilities through automated webpage interaction fuzzing and URL component synthesis.

中文: 通过自动化网页交互模糊测试和 URL 组件合成检测基于 DOM 的 XSS 漏洞。

6. Anota: Identifying Business Logic Vulnerabilities

Authors: Meng Wang et al. (CISPA, Max Planck, UESTC)

English: Identifies business logic vulnerabilities via annotation-based sanitization in web applications.

中文: 通过 Web 应用中基于注释的清理识别业务逻辑漏洞。

7. Bullseye: Detecting Prototype Pollution in NPM Packages

Authors: Tariq Houis et al. (Concordia University)

English: Detects prototype pollution vulnerabilities in NPM packages with proof-of-concept exploits.

中文: 检测 NPM 包中的原型污染漏洞并提供概念验证利用。

四、电子邮件安全

8. CoordMail: SMTP Timeout Coordination for Amplification Attacks

Authors: Ruixuan Li et al. (Tsinghua, Zhongguancun Lab, Coremail)

English: Exploits SMTP timeout and command interaction to coordinate email middleware for convergence amplification attacks.

中文: 利用 SMTP 超时和命令交互协调电子邮件中间件进行收敛放大攻击。

五、Wi-Fi 与无线安全

9. AirSnitch: Breaking Client Isolation in Wi-Fi Networks

Authors: Xin’an Zhou et al. (UC Riverside, KU Leuven)

English: Demystifies and breaks Wi-Fi client isolation mechanisms, exposing vulnerabilities in public Wi-Fi security.

中文: 揭秘并打破 Wi-Fi 客户端隔离机制，暴露公共 Wi-Fi 安全漏洞。

六、网络测量与分析

10. Aliens Among Us: Observing Private IPs on the Public Internet

Authors: Radu Anghel et al. (TU Delft, ICANN, RIPE NCC, CAIDA)

English: Measurement study observing prevalence of private/reserved IP addresses on the public internet and their security implications.

中文: 测量研究观察公共互联网上私有/保留 IP 地址的流行度及其安全影响。

11. Enhancing Website Fingerprinting Attacks Against Traffic Drift

Authors: Xinhao Deng et al. (Tsinghua University, Ant Group)

English: Enhances website fingerprinting attacks to remain effective even when traffic patterns change over time.

中文: 增强网站指纹攻击以在流量模式随时间变化时保持有效。

12. Cease at the Ultimate Goodness: Efficient Website Fingerprinting Defense

Authors: Rong Wang et al. (Southeast University, UMass Lowell)

English: Efficient website fingerprinting defense via iterative mutual information minimization.

中文: 通过迭代互信息最小化实现高效的网站指纹防御。

七、网络隐私

13. Beyond RTT: Adversarially Robust Residential Proxy Detection

Authors: Temoor Ali et al. (Qatar Computing Research Institute, HBKU)

English: Adversarially robust two-tiered approach for residential proxy detection beyond simple RTT measurements.

中文: 超越简单 RTT 测量的对抗鲁棒双层住宅代理检测方法。

14. Connecting the Dots: Linking Private Data Across Messaging Apps

Authors: Junkyu Kang et al. (KAIST, University of Maryland)

English: Investigative study on linking private user data across different messaging applications.

中文: 跨不同消息应用链接私有用户数据的调查研究。

八、网络协议与标准

15. ACTS: Attestations of Contents in TLS Sessions

Authors: Pierpaolo Della Monica et al. (Sapienza University of Rome)

English: Enables attestations of contents in TLS sessions for verifiable secure communications.

中文: 为 TLS 会话中的内容启用证明以实现可验证的安全通信。

16. CTng: Secure Certificate and Revocation Transparency

Authors: Jie Kong et al. (University of Connecticut, et al.)

English: Improves certificate transparency with stronger security guarantees against malicious certificate authorities.

中文: 通过针对恶意证书颁发机构的更强安全保证改进证书透明度。

研究趋势

🌐 互联网基础设施

  1. RPKI 部署与安全: 全球 RPKI 部署增加,但底层基础设施威胁不容忽视
  2. DNS 仍是攻击向量: 缓存时序攻击等新型 DNS 利用方式持续出现
  3. 证书透明度进化: CT 日志安全性和可靠性持续改进

🔒 Web 安全

  1. 可解释性需求: 从黑盒检测转向可解释的威胁分析
  2. 业务逻辑漏洞: 超越传统注入攻击,关注业务逻辑缺陷
  3. 供应链安全: NPM、开源包中的漏洞成为重点
  4. DOM-XSS 持续: 客户端漏洞仍然是主要威胁

📧 电子邮件与通信

  1. 协议级攻击: SMTP 等老协议的新型利用方式
  2. 放大攻击: 利用协议机制实现流量放大

📶 无线网络

  1. 隔离机制失效: Wi-Fi 客户端隔离等安全边界被突破
  2. 隐私威胁: 网站指纹、流量分析等隐私攻击持续演进

💡 技术洞察

  • 基础设施脆弱性: 互联网关键基础设施(路由、DNS、PKI)仍存在系统性风险
  • 隐私与安全矛盾: 网站指纹等攻击凸显隐私保护的挑战
  • 协议老化问题: SMTP、BACnet 等老协议难以修复
  • 测量研究价值: 大规模测量研究揭示真实世界的安全态势

🏢 实际影响

企业:

  • Web 应用安全检测工具的可解释性需求
  • 供应链安全(NPM、开源包)管理

ISP/运营商:

  • RPKI 部署和基础设施安全
  • DNS 服务的抗攻击能力

用户隐私:

  • 网站指纹威胁浏览隐私
  • DNS 缓存攻击泄露行为

公共场所:

  • Wi-Fi 客户端隔离失效影响咖啡馆、机场等场所安全

其他视角:

  • AI 与机器学习安全视角
  • 系统安全视角
  • IoT 与嵌入式系统视角
  • 密码学与隐私视角
