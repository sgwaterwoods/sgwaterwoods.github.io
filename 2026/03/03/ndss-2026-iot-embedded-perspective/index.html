NDSS 2026 论文精选 - IoT 与嵌入式系统视角

从 IoT/嵌入式视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖固件安全、工业控制、自主系统、无线通信

📄 Complete Paper List with Download Links: NDSS 2026 Accepted Papers

Perspective: IoT & Embedded Systems | Focus: Firmware, ICS, Automotive, Wireless

一、固件安全与分析

1. FirmAgent: LLM-Assisted IoT Firmware Vulnerability Discovery

Authors: Jiangan Ji et al. (Information Engineering University, Tsinghua)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Combines fuzzing with LLM agents for enhanced IoT firmware vulnerability discovery through intelligent analysis.

中文: 结合模糊测试与 LLM 代理通过智能分析增强物联网固件漏洞发现。

2. FirmCross: Detecting Taint Vulnerabilities in C-Lua Hybrid Services

Authors: Runhao Liu et al. (NUDT, Fudan University)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Detects taint-style vulnerabilities in modern firmware using hybrid C-Lua web services, common in routers and IoT devices.

中文: 检测使用混合 C-Lua Web 服务的现代固件中的污点式漏洞，常见于路由器和物联网设备。

二、工业控制系统 (ICS) 安全

3. LLM-Driven Fuzzing for Detecting PLC Logic Bugs

Authors: Jiaxing Cheng et al. (IIE CAS, NJUST, Virginia Tech)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Leverages LLMs to drive PLC fuzzing, detecting critical logic instruction bugs in industrial control systems.

中文: 利用 LLM 驱动 PLC 模糊测试，检测工业控制系统中的关键逻辑指令错误。

4. Discovering Blind-Trust Vulnerabilities in PLC Binaries

Authors: Fangzhou Dong et al. (Arizona State University, New Mexico State)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Discovers blind-trust vulnerabilities in PLC binaries via state machine recovery, revealing critical ICS flaws.

中文: 通过状态机恢复发现 PLC 二进制文件中的盲信任漏洞，揭示关键 ICS 缺陷。

5. BACnet or “BADnet”? Security of Implicitly Reserved Fields

Authors: Qiguang Zhang et al. (Southeast University, Shandong University)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Exposes security vulnerabilities in BACnet protocol by exploiting implicitly reserved fields, threatening smart buildings.

中文: 通过利用隐式保留字段暴露 BACnet 协议中的安全漏洞，威胁智能建筑。

三、自主系统与机器人安全

6. ADGFUZZ: Assignment Dependency-Guided Fuzzing for Robotic Vehicles

Authors: Yuncheng Wang et al. (IIE CAS, Ant Group, Tsinghua)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Assignment dependency-guided fuzzing for robotic vehicles, significantly improving bug discovery in autonomous systems.

中文: 针对机器人车辆的赋值依赖引导模糊测试，显著提高自动系统中的错误发现率。

7. FlyTrap: Physical Distance-Pulling Attack on Camera Tracking

Authors: [Full author list from NDSS]

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Demonstrates physical distance-pulling attacks against camera-based autonomous tracking systems via adversarial patterns.

中文: 通过对抗模式展示针对基于相机的自主跟踪系统的物理距离拉动攻击。

四、汽车安全

8. Automating Function-Level TARA for Automotive Security

Authors: Yuqiao Yang et al. (UESTC, GoGoByte Technology)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Automates function-level TARA (Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment) for automotive full-lifecycle security.

中文: 自动化汽车全生命周期安全的功能级威胁分析和风险评估 (TARA)。

五、无线通信安全

9. BLERP: BLE Re-Pairing Attacks and Defenses

Authors: Tommaso Sacchetti, Daniele Antonioli (EURECOM)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Identifies BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) re-pairing vulnerabilities and proposes effective defense mechanisms.

中文: 识别 BLE (低功耗蓝牙) 重新配对漏洞并提出有效的防御机制。

10. BSFuzzer: Context-Aware Semantic Fuzzing for BLE Logic Flaws

Authors: Ting Yang et al. (Xidian University, Kanazawa University, et al.)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Context-aware semantic fuzzing specifically designed to detect BLE logic flaws in IoT devices.

中文: 专门设计用于检测物联网设备中 BLE 逻辑缺陷的上下文感知语义模糊测试。

11. ANONYCALL: Enabling Native Private Calling in Mobile Networks

Authors: Hexuan Yu et al. (Virginia Tech, University of Kentucky, Georgia Tech)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Enables native private calling in mobile networks without requiring additional infrastructure or apps.

中文: 在移动网络中启用原生私密呼叫，无需额外基础设施或应用。

12. DualStrike: Accurate Real-time Keystroke Eavesdropping on Keyboards

Authors: Xiaomeng Chen et al. (Shanghai Jiao Tong University, UC Irvine)

📄 Paper: NDSS 2026

English: Accurate, real-time eavesdropping and injection of keystrokes on commodity keyboards via electromagnetic emanations.

中文: 通过电磁辐射对商品键盘进行准确、实时的按键窃听和注入。

研究趋势

🎯 核心方向

  1. AI 赋能固件分析: LLM 应用于固件漏洞发现和逆向工程
  2. 工业控制系统安全: PLC、SCADA、BACnet 等关键基础设施
  3. 自主系统攻击: 机器人、无人机、自动驾驶的物理对抗攻击
  4. 无线协议漏洞: BLE、蜂窝网络等无线通信协议的安全问题
  5. 跨语言边界: C-Lua 混合系统中的污点传播和边界安全

💡 技术洞察

  • 固件复杂性增加: 现代固件使用多种语言和运行时，增加攻击面
  • 物理攻击可行性: 对抗样本、电磁攻击等物理攻击威胁增大
  • 状态机重要性: 状态机恢复成为理解和攻击嵌入式系统的关键
  • 无线通信漏洞持续: BLE、4G/5G 等协议持续发现新漏洞

🏭 实际影响

工业领域:

  • 工厂自动化、电力系统、水处理等关键基础设施
  • PLC 和 SCADA 系统的安全加固需求

汽车领域:

  • 自动驾驶系统的安全威胁分析
  • 车载网络和控制系统的漏洞防护

智能家居:

  • 路由器、智能门锁、安防系统的固件安全
  • BLE 设备的配对和通信安全

移动通信:

  • 蜂窝网络中的隐私保护
  • 键盘、输入设备的电磁泄露防护

⚠️ 安全挑战

  1. 生命周期长: IoT 设备和工业系统使用寿命长，难以及时更新
  2. 资源受限: 嵌入式设备计算能力有限，安全机制难以部署
  3. 异构性强: 各种架构、操作系统、通信协议增加安全分析难度
  4. 物理可达性: 物理攻击(如 FlyTrap、DualStrike)难以完全防御
