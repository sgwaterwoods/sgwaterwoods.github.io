1. MinBucket MPSI: Breaking the Max-Size Bottleneck

Authors: Binbin Tu, Boyudong Zhu, Yang Cao, Yu Chen (Shandong University)

Abstract

Multi-Party Private Set Intersection (Cardinality) protocol enables $T$ $(T > 2)$ parties, each holding a private set, to jointly compute the intersection (or its cardinality) without revealing any additional information to other parties. To date, all known MPSI (MPSI-Card) protocols require communication complexity that scales linearly with the size of the large set, fundamentally precluding their efficient deployment in real-world applications with heterogeneous input scales.

In this work, we present a new framework for MPSI based on newly proposed protocols: batched membership conditional randomness generation and joint private equality test. By instantiating this framework, we develop two MPSI protocols with communication complexities that are linear in the size of the small set and logarithmic in the size of the large set. One protocol offers security against an arbitrary number of colluding parties, while the other secures against $(T-2)$ colluding parties. Additionally, we develop a protocol called the joint permuted private equality test and propose the MPSI-Card framework. By instantiating this framework, we derive an MPSI-Card protocol with similar communication efficiency: linear in the small set and logarithmic in the large set, providing security against an arbitrary number of colluding parties.

We implement our protocols and conduct extensive experiments over both LAN and WAN networks. Experimental results demonstrate that our protocols achieve significantly better performance as the size difference between the sets or the number of participants holding the small set increases. For the setting, where $5$ parties holding large set (size $2^{20}$) and $5$ parties holding small set (size $2^{10}$) with a single thread and a $10$ Mbps bandwidth, our MPSI (MPSI-Card) protocol requires only $12.2$ ($12.2$) MB of communication and $129.86$ ($130.05$) seconds of runtime. Compared with the state-of-the-art MPSI by Wu et al. (USENIX Security 2024) and MPSI-Card by Gao et al. (PETS 2024), our protocol achieves a $157\times$ $(76\times)$ reduction in communication cost and a $12.7\times$ $(3.1\times)$ speedup in runtime.

摘要

多方隐私集合交集(基数)协议使得$T$($T > 2$)方各自持有私有集合，能够联合计算交集(或其基数)而不向其他方透露任何额外信息。迄今为止，所有已知的MPSI(MPSI-Card)协议都需要与大集合大小线性相关的通信复杂度，从根本上阻止了它们在具有异构输入规模的真实世界应用中的有效部署。

在这项工作中，我们基于新提出的协议提出了一个新的MPSI框架：批量成员条件随机性生成和联合私有相等性测试。通过实例化这个框架，我们开发了两个MPSI协议，其通信复杂度与小集合大小线性相关，与大集合大小对数相关。一个协议提供针对任意数量串通方的安全性，而另一个协议针对$(T-2)$串通方提供安全性。此外，我们开发了一个称为联合排列私有相等性测试的协议，并提出了MPSI-Card框架。通过实例化这个框架，我们推导出一个具有类似通信效率的MPSI-Card协议：与小集合线性相关，与大集合对数相关，提供针对任意数量串通方的安全性。

我们实现了我们的协议，并在LAN和WAN网络上进行了广泛的实验。实验结果表明，随着集合之间大小差异或持有小集合的参与者数量增加，我们的协议取得了显著更好的性能。对于5方持有大集合(大小$2^{20}$)和5方持有小集合(大小$2^{10}$)，使用单线程和10 Mbps带宽的设置，我们的MPSI(MPSI-Card)协议仅需要12.2(12.2)MB通信和129.86(130.05)秒运行时间。与Wu等人(USENIX Security 2024)的最先进MPSI和Gao等人(PETS 2024)的MPSI-Card相比，我们的协议在通信成本方面实现了157倍(76倍)的降低，在运行时间方面实现了12.7倍(3.1倍)的加速。

2. Faster Than Ever: Lightweight Private Set Intersection

Authors: Guowei Ling, Peng Tang, Liyao Xiang, Weidong Qiu (Shanghai Jiao Tong), Jinyong Shan (Beijing Smartchip Microelectronics)

Abstract

In this work, we present a new lightweight two-party Private Set Intersection (PSI) paradigm in both the semi-honest and malicious models. It requires only a small number of base OTs and a single Oblivious Key-Value Stores (OKVS) encoding and decoding. All computations (except for the base OTs) can be implemented using SIMD-accelerated symmetric cryptographic instructions and efficient bitwise operations. Furthermore, we extend the proposed PSI protocol to circuit PSI and, subsequently, to several PSI variants, including PSI-cardinality, PSI-sum, and Private Join and Compute (PJC).

All proposed protocols are evaluated under both LAN and WAN settings, with performance compared against existing works. Experimental results show that the proposed PSI outperforms the most efficient VOLE-based PSI by approximately 40% in runtime, while consistently incurring lower communication overhead under the same settings. For circuit PSI, it is up to $3.7\times$ faster and reduces communication by a factor of $1.5$ compared to VOLE-based circuit PSI constructions. In the cases of PSI-cardinality and PSI-sum, it achieves speedups of up to $12.4\times$ and $10\times$, respectively, while incurring only moderate communication overhead. For PJC, the proposed protocol outperforms prior work by $762\times$ in runtime and achieves a $3.2\times$ reduction in communication, maintaining high efficiency even under a low-bandwidth condition.

摘要

在这项工作中，我们在半诚实和恶意模型中提出了一个新的轻量级双方隐私集合交集(PSI)范式。它仅需要少量基础OT和单个遗忘键值存储(OKVS)编码和解码。所有计算(除了基础OT)都可以使用SIMD加速的对称加密指令和高效的位运算来实现。此外，我们将提出的PSI协议扩展到电路PSI，随后扩展到几个PSI变体，包括PSI基数、PSI求和和私有连接与计算(PJC)。

所有提出的协议都在LAN和WAN设置下进行评估，性能与现有工作进行比较。实验结果表明，提出的PSI在运行时间上优于最高效的基于VOLE的PSI约40%，同时在相同设置下始终产生较低的通信开销。对于电路PSI，与基于VOLE的电路PSI构造相比，它快达3.7倍，并将通信减少了1.5倍。在PSI基数和PSI求和的情况下，它分别实现了高达12.4倍和10倍的加速，同时仅产生适度的通信开销。对于PJC，提出的协议在运行时间上优于先前工作762倍，并实现了3.2倍的通信减少，即使在低带宽条件下也能保持高效率。

3. cwPSU: Efficient Unbalanced Private Set Union

Authors: Qingwen Li (Xidian University), Song Bian (Beihang University), Hui Li (Xidian University)

Abstract

Private Set Union (PSU) allows two parties to compute the union of their private sets without revealing any additional information. While several PSU protocols have been proposed for the unbalanced setting, these constructions still suffer from substantial communication overhead as the size of the larger set increases. Moreover, their reliance on multiple invocations of oblivious pseudo-random functions results in increased communication rounds, which becomes a practical bottleneck.

In this work, we present cwPSU, a novel unbalanced PSU protocol built upon constant-weight codes and leveled fully homomorphic encryption. To prevent leakage, we introduce a new technique called Batched Ciphertext Shuffle, which enables secure reordering of packed ciphertexts. Additionally, we propose an optimized arithmetic constant-weight equality operator, which reduces the number of non-scalar multiplications to just one-third of those required by the naïve approach. The communication complexity of our protocol scales linearly with the size of the smaller set and remains independent of the larger set. Notably, cwPSU requires only a single round of online communication.

Experimental results demonstrate that our cwPSU outperforms the state-of-the-art protocol in various network conditions, achieving a $5.1$–$32.4\times$ reduction in communication and a $3.1$–$13.3\times$ speedup in runtime.

摘要

隐私集合并集(PSU)允许双方计算其私有集合的并集而不透露任何额外信息。虽然已经为不平衡设置提出了几个PSU协议，但随着较大集合大小的增加，这些构造仍然遭受大量通信开销的困扰。此外，它们依赖于遗忘伪随机函数的多次调用导致通信轮次增加，这成为一个实际瓶颈。

在这项工作中，我们提出了cwPSU，这是一个基于常重码和分级完全同态加密构建的新型不平衡PSU协议。为了防止泄漏，我们引入了一种称为批量密文洗牌的新技术，它能够安全地重新排序打包的密文。此外，我们提出了一个优化的算术常重相等性运算符，它将非标量乘法的数量减少到朴素方法所需数量的仅三分之一。我们协议的通信复杂度与较小集合的大小线性相关，并且与较大集合无关。值得注意的是，cwPSU仅需要一轮在线通信。

实验结果表明，我们的cwPSU在各种网络条件下优于最先进的协议，实现了5.1至32.4倍的通信减少和3.1至13.3倍的运行时间加速。

4. BKPIR: Keyword PIR for Private Boolean Retrieval

Authors: Jie Song, Zhen Xu, Yan Zhang (IIE, CAS), Pengwei Zhan (Sangfor Technologies), Mingxuan Li (PPSUC), Shuai Ma (Beihang), Ru Xie (IIE, CAS)

Abstract

Keyword Private Information Retrieval (Keyword PIR) enables users to retrieve data associated with specific keywords from a database while keeping their queries private. However, existing Keyword PIR schemes struggle to support the boolean retrieval model, which is essential for practical applications that require logical combinations of terms.

This paper proposes a novel keyword PIR scheme leveraging advancements in homomorphic equality operations. It enables privacy-preserving retrieval over databases with many-to-many keyword-value mappings while supporting boolean operators for expressive search logic. Importantly, this extension preserves the core security guarantees of classical PIR. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first work to integrate keyword PIR with the boolean retrieval model.

Experimental evaluation shows that our scheme achieves a communication cost reduction proportional to the total number of values in the many-to-many keyword-value database, along with aggregate query processing performance gains that scale linearly with the number of values. These improvements enhance its feasibility for real-world applications such as privacy-preserving web search and patent retrieval.

摘要

关键词隐私信息检索(Keyword PIR)使用户能够从数据库中检索与特定关键词相关的数据，同时保持其查询的私密性。然而，现有的Keyword PIR方案难以支持布尔检索模型，这对于需要术语逻辑组合的实际应用至关重要。

本文提出了一种利用同态相等性操作进展的新型关键词PIR方案。它在具有多对多关键词-值映射的数据库上实现隐私保护检索，同时支持用于表达性搜索逻辑的布尔运算符。重要的是，这种扩展保留了经典PIR的核心安全保证。据我们所知，这是第一个将关键词PIR与布尔检索模型集成的工作。

实验评估表明，我们的方案实现了与多对多关键词-值数据库中值总数成比例的通信成本降低，以及与值数量线性相关的聚合查询处理性能提升。这些改进增强了其在真实世界应用(如隐私保护Web搜索和专利检索)中的可行性。

5. CryptPEFT: Private Neural Network Inference via PEFT

Authors: Saisai Xia, Wenhao Wang (IIE, CAS), Zihao Wang (NTU), Yuhui Zhang (IIE, CAS), Yier Jin (USTC), Dan Meng, Rui Hou (IIE, CAS)

Abstract

Publicly available large pretrained models (i.e., backbones) and lightweight adapters for parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) have become standard components in modern machine learning pipelines. However, preserving the privacy of both user inputs and fine-tuned adapters—often trained on sensitive data—during inference remains a significant challenge. Applying cryptographic techniques, such as multi-party computation (MPC), to PEFT settings still incurs substantial encrypted computation across both the backbone and adapter, mainly due to the inherent two-way communication between them.

To address this limitation, we propose CryptPEFT, the first PEFT solution specifically designed for private inference scenarios. CryptPEFT introduces a novel one-way communication (OWC) architecture that confines encrypted computation solely to the adapter, significantly reducing both computational and communication overhead. To maintain strong model utility under this constraint, we explore the design space of OWC-compatible adapters and employ an automated architecture search algorithm to optimize the trade-off between private inference efficiency and model utility.

We evaluated CryptPEFT using Vision Transformer backbones across widely used image classification datasets. Our results show that CryptPEFT significantly outperforms existing baselines, delivering speedups ranging from $20.62\times$ to $291.48\times$ in simulated wide-area network (WAN) and local-area network (LAN) settings. On CIFAR-100, CryptPEFT attains 85.47% accuracy with just 2.26 seconds of inference latency. These findings demonstrate that CryptPEFT offers an efficient and privacy-preserving solution for modern PEFT-based inference.

摘要

公开可用的大型预训练模型(即骨干)和用于参数高效微调(PEFT)的轻量级适配器已成为现代机器学习管道中的标准组件。然而，在推理期间保护用户输入和经常在敏感数据上训练的微调适配器的隐私仍然是一个重大挑战。将加密技术(如多方计算MPC)应用于PEFT设置仍然会在骨干和适配器上产生大量加密计算，主要是由于它们之间固有的双向通信。

为了解决这一限制，我们提出了CryptPEFT，这是第一个专门为私有推理场景设计的PEFT解决方案。CryptPEFT引入了一种新颖的*单向通信(OWC)*架构，将加密计算仅限于适配器，显著减少了计算和通信开销。为了在这一约束下保持强大的模型效用，我们探索了OWC兼容适配器的设计空间，并采用自动化架构搜索算法来优化私有推理效率和模型效用之间的权衡。

我们在广泛使用的图像分类数据集上使用Vision Transformer骨干评估了CryptPEFT。我们的结果表明，CryptPEFT显著优于现有基线，在模拟广域网(WAN)和局域网(LAN)设置中实现了20.62倍至291.48倍的加速。在CIFAR-100上，CryptPEFT以仅2.26秒的推理延迟达到85.47%的准确率。这些发现表明CryptPEFT为现代基于PEFT的推理提供了一个高效且隐私保护的解决方案。

6. CRISP: Cryptographic Framework Against Malicious Clients

Authors: Xiaoyu Fang, Shihui Zheng, Lize Gu (Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications)

Abstract

Machine learning inference protocols based on semi-honest security models are vulnerable to attacks from malicious clients in real-world applications. These attacks can lead to the leakage of machine learning model parameters. Previous works introduced additional MACs computations to ensure correct client behavior. However, this resulted in higher runtime and communication costs during online inference.

In this work, we present CRISP, an efficient two-party cryptographic framework designed to defend against malicious clients. Specifically:

  1. We design protocols for non-linear layers based on a new cryptographic primitive (Function Secret Sharing). The core of our approach focuses on optimizing the reconstruction process of MACs.

  2. We propose a complex domain verification mechanism for linear layers. This mechanism eliminates the additional MACs computations by making better use of the complex space in homomorphic encryption CKKS.

Furthermore, in previous work (SIMC, USENIX Security’22), we identified compatibility issues in practical applications. The MAC reconstruction process in the nonlinear layers may leak intermediate inputs and outputs of the model when certain garbled circuit optimizations are applied. In contrast, CRISP effectively avoids this problem.

In secure inference benchmarks considered in SIMC, CRISP reduces the total communication cost of ML inference by up to 94% and cuts inference latency by up to 43%.

摘要

基于半诚实安全模型的机器学习推理协议在真实世界应用中容易受到恶意客户端的攻击。这些攻击可能导致机器学习模型参数的泄漏。先前的工作引入了额外的MAC计算以确保正确的客户端行为。然而，这导致在线推理期间更高的运行时间和通信成本。

在这项工作中，我们提出了CRISP，这是一个旨在防御恶意客户端的高效双方加密框架。具体而言：

1)我们基于新的加密原语(函数秘密共享)设计了非线性层的协议。我们方法的核心集中在优化MAC的重建过程。

2)我们为线性层提出了复杂域验证机制。这种机制通过更好地利用同态加密CKKS中的复杂空间来消除额外的MAC计算。

此外，在先前的工作(SIMC，USENIX Security’22)中，我们识别了实际应用中的兼容性问题。当应用某些混淆电路优化时，非线性层中的MAC重建过程可能会泄漏模型的中间输入和输出。相比之下，CRISP有效地避免了这个问题。

在SIMC中考虑的安全推理基准测试中，CRISP将ML推理的总通信成本降低了高达94%，并将推理延迟降低了高达43%。

7. Cryptobazaar: Private Sealed-bid Auctions at Scale

Authors: Andrija Novakovic, Kobi Gurkan (Bain Capital Crypto), Alireza Kavousi, Philipp Jovanovic (UCL)

Abstract

This work introduces Cryptobazaar, a scalable, private, and decentralized sealed-bid auction protocol. In particular, our protocol protects the privacy of losing bidders by preserving the confidentiality of their bids while ensuring public verifiability of the outcome and relying only on a single untrusted auctioneer for coordination. At its core, Cryptobazaar combines an efficient distributed protocol to compute the logical-OR for a list of unary-encoded bids with various novel zero-knowledge succinct arguments of knowledge that may be of independent interest.

We present protocol variants that can be used for efficient first-, second-, and more generally (p+1)st-price as well as sequential first-price auctions. Finally, the performance evaluation of our Cryptobazaar implementation shows that it is highly practical. For example, a single auction run with 128 bidders and a price range of 1024 values terminates in less than 0.5sec and requires each bidder to send and receive only about 32KB of data.

摘要

这项工作介绍了Cryptobazaar，这是一个可扩展、私密且去中心化的密封投标拍卖协议。特别是，我们的协议通过保留失败投标人出价的机密性来保护其隐私，同时确保结果的公开可验证性，并仅依赖单个不受信任的拍卖人进行协调。其核心是，Cryptobazaar结合了一个高效的分布式协议，用于计算一元编码出价列表的逻辑或，以及各种新颖的零知识简洁知识论证，这些论证可能具有独立的价值。

我们提出了可用于高效第一价格、第二价格以及更一般的(p+1)价格以及顺序第一价格拍卖的协议变体。最后，我们Cryptobazaar实现的性能评估表明它非常实用。例如，一次拍卖运行有128个投标人和1024个价格值的范围在不到0.5秒内终止，并且要求每个投标人仅发送和接收约32KB的数据。

8. Cirrus: Performant and Accountable Distributed SNARK

Authors: Wenhao Wang (Yale, IC3), Fangyan Shi (Tsinghua), Dani Vilardell (Cornell Tech, IC3), Fan Zhang (Yale, IC3)

Abstract

Succinct Non-interactive Arguments of Knowledge (SNARKs) can enable efficient verification of computation in many applications. However, generating SNARK proofs for large-scale tasks, such as verifiable machine learning or virtual machines, remains computationally expensive. A promising approach is to distribute the proof generation workload across multiple workers. A practical distributed SNARK protocol should have three properties: horizontal scalability with low overhead (linear computation and logarithmic communication per worker), accountability (efficient detection of malicious workers), and a universal trusted setup independent of circuits and the number of workers. Existing protocols fail to achieve all these properties.

In this paper, we present Cirrus, the first distributed SNARK generation protocol achieving all three desirable properties at once. Our protocol builds on HyperPlonk (EUROCRYPT’23), inheriting its universal trusted setup. It achieves linear computation complexity for both workers and the coordinator, along with low communication overhead. To achieve accountability, we introduce a highly efficient accountability protocol to localize malicious workers. Additionally, we propose a hierarchical aggregation technique to further reduce the coordinator’s workload.

We implemented and evaluated Cirrus on machines with modest hardware. Our experiments show that Cirrus is highly scalable: it generates proofs for circuits with $33$M gates in under $40$ seconds using $32$ $8$-core machines. Compared to the state-of-the-art accountable protocol Hekaton (CCS’24), Cirrus achieves over $7\times$ faster proof generation for PLONK-friendly circuits such as the Pedersen hash. Our accountability protocol also efficiently identifies faulty workers within just $4$ seconds, making Cirrus particularly suitable for decentralized and outsourced computation scenarios.

摘要

简洁非交互式知识论证(SNARK)可以在许多应用中实现计算的高效验证。然而，为大规模任务(如可验证机器学习或虚拟机)生成SNARK证明仍然在计算上很昂贵。一个有前途的方法是将证明生成工作负载分布在多个工作者之间。实用的分布式SNARK协议应该具有三个属性：低开销的水平可扩展性(每个工作者的线性计算和对数通信)、问责制(高效检测恶意工作者)以及独立于电路和工作者数量的通用可信设置。现有协议无法同时实现所有这些属性。

在本文中，我们提出了Cirrus，这是第一个同时实现所有三个理想属性的分布式SNARK生成协议。我们的协议基于HyperPlonk(EUROCRYPT’23)，继承了其通用可信设置。它为工作者和协调器实现了线性计算复杂度，以及低通信开销。为了实现问责制，我们引入了一个高效的问责协议来定位恶意工作者。此外，我们提出了一种分层聚合技术以进一步减少协调器的工作负载。

我们在具有适度硬件的机器上实现并评估了Cirrus。我们的实验表明Cirrus具有高度可扩展性：它在不到40秒的时间内使用32台8核机器为具有3300万个门的电路生成证明。与最先进的可问责协议Hekaton(CCS’24)相比，Cirrus为Pedersen哈希等PLONK友好电路实现了超过7倍的更快证明生成。我们的问责协议还在仅4秒内高效识别故障工作者，使Cirrus特别适合分散式和外包计算场景。

三、差分隐私

9. Augmented Shuffle Differential Privacy for Large-Domain Data

Authors: Takao Murakami (ISM/AIST/RIKEN AIP), Yuichi Sei (UEC), Reo Eriguchi (AIST)

Abstract

Shuffle DP (Differential Privacy) protocols provide high accuracy and privacy by introducing a shuffler who randomly shuffles data in a distributed system. However, most shuffle DP protocols are vulnerable to two attacks: collusion attacks by the data collector and users and data poisoning attacks. A recent study addresses this issue by introducing an augmented shuffle DP protocol, where users do not add noise and the shuffler performs random sampling and dummy data addition. However, it focuses on frequency estimation over categorical data with a small domain and cannot be applied to a large domain due to prohibitively high communication and computational costs.

In this paper, we fill this gap by introducing a novel augmented shuffle DP protocol called the FME (Filtering-with-Multiple-Encryption) protocol. Our FME protocol uses a hash function to filter out unpopular items and then accurately calculates frequencies for popular items. To perform this within one round of interaction between users and the shuffler, our protocol carefully communicates within a system using multiple encryption. We also apply our FME protocol to more advanced KV (Key-Value) statistics estimation with an additional technique to reduce bias.

For both categorical and KV data, we prove that our protocol provides computational DP, high robustness to the above two attacks, accuracy, and efficiency. We show the effectiveness of our proposals through comparisons with twelve existing protocols.

摘要

Experimental results validate that ANStega simultaneously achieves optimal embedding capacity, optimal efficiency (embedding complexity with $O(1)$) and optimal security, successfully resolving the long-standing trilemma in generative steganography.

摘要

生成式隐写术显示出巨大的隐蔽通信前景，但现有方法通常受到容量、效率和安全性三难困境的限制。基于霍夫曼编码(HC)的方法遭受效率和安全性差的困扰，而基于算术编码(AC)的方法尽管实现了最佳容量，但也带来了安全风险。虽然最近可证明安全的方法已经解决了安全问题，但它们通常以提高嵌入复杂性或降低容量为代价——未能匹配基于AC的方法展示的高容量。

为了解决这个三难困境，我们将非对称数字系统(ANS)应用于隐写术。我们的核心洞察力是重新利用ANS状态机，使用其解码函数进行嵌入，使用其编码函数进行提取。为了将这个概念转化为实用系统，我们引入了几个关键创新。首先，我们结合了具有状态重新规范化的流式架构，以实现任意长消息的稳定嵌入。其次，我们采用直接浮点算术，避免代价高昂的概率到频率转换以减少复杂性和精度损失。

更关键的是，我们引入了一个创新的加密掩码机制，确保采样过程由加密安全的伪随机数生成器驱动，从而实现可证明的安全性。最后，通过将核心计算优化为高效的位移操作，ANStega实现了卓越的嵌入和提取速度。

实验结果验证了ANStega同时实现了最佳嵌入容量、最佳效率(嵌入复杂度为$O(1)$)和最佳安全性，成功解决了生成式隐写术中长期存在的三难困境。

研究趋势总结

NDSS 2026 密码学/隐私研究呈现六大趋势:

  1. 隐私保护计算实用化 - PSI/PSU/PIR协议效率大幅提升，支持异构规模和布尔检索
  2. 安全多方计算优化 - 针对恶意模型的高效协议、单向通信架构、PEFT隐私推理
  3. 差分隐私增强 - 洗牌DP扩展到大域数据、GNN隐私预算收敛、LLM提示消毒
  4. 区块链共识创新 - 混合故障模型、跨链通信原语、分布式SNARK加速
  5. PKI与消息系统 - 抗滥用可追溯性、多方证书透明度、分布式广播加密
  6. 隐写术突破 - ANS实现容量/效率/安全三位一体最优
