NDSS 2026 论文精选 - 密码学与隐私视角

从密码学/隐私视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖加密协议、隐私保护、零知识证明、区块链安全

Perspective: Cryptography & Privacy | Focus: Encryption, PIR, ZKP, Blockchain, Privacy-Preserving ML

一、私有信息检索 (PIR)

1. BKPIR: Keyword PIR for Private Boolean Retrieval

Authors: Jie Song et al. (IIE CAS, Sichuan Police College, et al.)

English: Efficient keyword-based private information retrieval for Boolean retrieval, enabling private database queries.

中文: 用于布尔检索的高效基于关键字的私有信息检索,实现私有数据库查询。

二、加密消息系统

2. Abuse Resistant Traceability for Encrypted Messaging Systems

Authors: Zhongming Wang et al. (Chongqing University, SMU, NTU)

English: Abuse-resistant traceability mechanism for encrypted messaging with minimal trust, balancing privacy and accountability.

中文: 具有最小信任的加密消息系统的抗滥用可追溯机制,平衡隐私和问责。

三、隐私保护计算

3. Cryptobazaar: Private Sealed-bid Auctions at Scale

Authors: Andrija Novakovic et al. (Bain Capital Crypto, UCL)

English: Enables large-scale private sealed-bid auctions using cryptographic techniques while maintaining auction integrity.

中文: 使用密码学技术实现大规模私有密封投标拍卖,同时保持拍卖完整性。

4. CryptPEFT: Efficient Private Neural Network Inference

Authors: Saisai Xia et al. (IIE CAS, NTU, USTC)

English: Efficient and private neural network inference via parameter-efficient fine-tuning with cryptographic protection.

中文: 通过参数高效微调和密码学保护实现高效私密的神经网络推理。

5. cwPSU: Efficient Unbalanced Private Set Union

Authors: Qingwen Li et al. (Xidian University, Beihang University)

English: Efficient solution for unbalanced private set union using constant-weight codes, improving performance dramatically.

中文: 使用常权码的不平衡私有集合并集的高效解决方案,显著提高性能。

6. Faster Than Ever: New Lightweight Private Set Intersection

Authors: Guowei Ling et al. (Shanghai Jiaotong University)

English: Record-breaking lightweight PSI protocol enabling practical privacy-preserving data analysis at scale.

中文: 破纪录的轻量级 PSI 协议,实现大规模实用隐私保护数据分析。

四、零知识证明与区块链

7. Cirrus: Performant and Accountable Distributed SNARK

Authors: Wenhao Wang et al. (Yale University, Tsinghua University, Cornell Tech)

English: Distributed SNARK system that is both performant and accountable for scalable zero-knowledge proof generation.

中文: 高性能且可问责的分布式 SNARK 系统,用于可扩展的零知识证明生成。

8. Distributed Broadcast Encryption for Private Blockchains

Authors: Angelo De Caro et al. (IBM Research Zurich, IBM Research India)

English: Enables confidential interoperability across private blockchains through distributed broadcast encryption.

中文: 通过分布式广播加密实现跨私有区块链的机密互操作性。

9. BunnyFinder: Finding Incentive Flaws for Ethereum Consensus

Authors: Rujia Li et al. (Tsinghua University, Shandong University, Ant Group)

English: Systematically finds incentive flaws in Ethereum consensus mechanisms that could lead to attacks.

中文: 系统性地发现以太坊共识机制中可能导致攻击的激励缺陷。

10. Cross-Consensus Reliable Broadcast and Applications

Authors: Yue Huang et al. (Tsinghua University)

English: Cross-consensus reliable broadcast protocol enabling secure communication across different blockchain systems.

中文: 跨共识可靠广播协议,实现跨不同区块链系统的安全通信。

11. Consensus in Known Participation Model with Byzantine Faults

Authors: Chenxu Wang et al. (Shandong University, Tsinghua University)

English: Consensus protocol for known participation model tolerating Byzantine faults and sleepy replicas.

中文: 已知参与模型的共识协议,容忍拜占庭故障和睡眠副本。

五、隐私保护机器学习

12. Convergent Privacy Framework for Multi-layer GNNs

Authors: Yu Zheng et al. (UC Irvine, UCSD)

English: Convergent privacy framework for multi-layer graph neural networks through contractive message passing.

中文: 通过收缩消息传递为多层图神经网络提供收敛隐私框架。

13. CRISP: Efficient Cryptographic ML Inference Against Malicious Clients

Authors: Xiaoyu Fang et al. (Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications)

English: Efficient cryptographic framework for ML inference protecting against malicious clients.

中文: 针对恶意客户端保护的高效密码学机器学习推理框架。

六、差分隐私

14. Augmented Shuffle Differential Privacy Protocols

Authors: Takao Murakami et al. (ISM, AIST, RIKEN AIP)

English: Augmented shuffle differential privacy protocols for large-domain categorical and key-value data.

中文: 用于大域分类和键值数据的增强洗牌差分隐私协议。

七、证书与认证

15. CTng: Secure Certificate and Revocation Transparency

Authors: Jie Kong et al. (University of Connecticut, et al.)

English: Secure certificate and revocation transparency system with stronger guarantees against malicious CAs.

中文: 具有针对恶意 CA 更强保证的安全证书和撤销透明度系统。

八、去中心化系统安全

16. CtPhishCapture: Uncovering Cryptocurrency Wallet Phishing

Authors: Hui Jiang et al. (Tsinghua, Baidu, Nankai University)

English: Uncovers credential-theft-based phishing scams targeting cryptocurrency wallets.

中文: 揭露针对加密货币钱包的基于凭证盗窃的钓鱼诈骗。

研究趋势

🔐 密码学实用化

  1. 性能突破: PIR、PSI 等隐私保护技术达到实用性能水平
  2. 轻量化设计: 面向资源受限环境的密码学协议
  3. 分布式 ZKP: 零知识证明从单点到分布式计算

⛓️ 区块链安全

  1. 跨链互操作: 不同区块链间的安全通信和价值转移
  2. 共识安全性: 持续发现和修复共识机制的激励缺陷
  3. 隐私保护: 区块链上的隐私保护技术(广播加密等)

🤖 隐私保护 ML

  1. 密态推理: 加密数据上的机器学习推理
  2. 联邦学习隐私: GNN 等新型模型的隐私保护
  3. 恶意客户端防护: 保护服务器免受恶意客户端攻击

📊 差分隐私进展

  1. Shuffle 模型: 洗牌差分隐私成为主流
  2. 大规模数据: 支持大域和高维数据的差分隐私

💡 技术洞察

从理论到实践的转变:

  • PIR、PSI 等技术性能达到可部署水平
  • 密码学与机器学习的深度融合
  • 区块链从概念验证到生产系统

隐私与效率的平衡:

  • 轻量化设计使隐私保护更实用
  • 参数高效微调(PEFT)与密码学结合
  • Constant-weight codes 等新技术提升效率

分布式密码学崛起:

  • 分布式 SNARK、跨链协议
  • 多方计算在实际场景中应用

🏢 实际应用

金融科技:

  • 私有拍卖系统
  • 加密货币钱包安全
  • 跨链资产转移

医疗健康:

  • 隐私保护的医疗数据分析(PIR、PSI)
  • 联邦学习在医疗 AI 中应用

云计算:

  • 加密数据上的 ML 推理
  • 隐私保护的数据共享

通信安全:

  • 端到端加密消息系统
  • 可追溯但抗滥用的通信

区块链:

  • DeFi 隐私保护
  • 跨链桥安全
  • 共识机制安全加固

⚡ 性能里程碑

  • PSI: “Faster Than Ever” 实现破纪录性能
  • PIR: 关键字 PIR 达到实用级别
  • ZKP: 分布式 SNARK 实现可扩展性

其他视角:

  • AI 与机器学习安全视角
  • 系统安全视角

NDSS 2026 论文精选 - 网络与基础设施视角

从网络/基础设施视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖互联网路由、DNS、Web 安全、电子邮件协议

Perspective: Network & Infrastructure Security | Focus: Routing, DNS, Web, Email, Wi-Fi

一、互联网路由安全 (RPKI)

1. Crack in the Armor: Infrastructure Threats to RPKI Publication Points

Authors: Yunhao Liu et al. (Tsinghua University, Fudan University)

English: Identifies underlying infrastructure threats to RPKI publication point reachability that could undermine internet routing security.

中文: 识别对 RPKI 发布点可达性的底层基础设施威胁，可能破坏互联网路由安全。

2. Demystifying RPKI-Invalid Prefixes: Hidden Causes and Risks

Authors: Weitong Li et al. (Virginia Tech, CableLabs)

English: Uncovers hidden causes of RPKI-invalid prefixes and analyzes their security risks in global routing systems.

中文: 揭示 RPKI 无效前缀的隐藏原因并分析其在全球路由系统中的安全风险。

二、DNS 与缓存攻击

3. Continuous User Behavior Monitoring via DNS Cache Timing Attacks

Authors: Hannes Weissteiner et al. (Graz University of Technology, University of Vienna)

English: Demonstrates continuous user behavior monitoring through DNS cache timing attacks without network access or malware.

中文: 展示通过 DNS 缓存时序攻击进行持续用户行为监控，无需网络访问或恶意软件。

三、Web 应用安全

4. Achieving Interpretable DL-based Web Attack Detection

Authors: Peiyang Li et al. (Tsinghua University, Ant Group, et al.)

English: Achieves interpretable deep learning-based web attack detection by localizing malicious payloads for analyst verification.

中文: 通过定位恶意载荷实现可解释的基于深度学习的 Web 攻击检测，供分析师验证。

5. DOM-XSS Detection via Webpage Interaction Fuzzing

Authors: Nuno Sabino et al. (CMU, IST Lisbon, et al.)

English: Detects DOM-based XSS vulnerabilities through automated webpage interaction fuzzing and URL component synthesis.

中文: 通过自动化网页交互模糊测试和 URL 组件合成检测基于 DOM 的 XSS 漏洞。

6. Anota: Identifying Business Logic Vulnerabilities

Authors: Meng Wang et al. (CISPA, Max Planck, UESTC)

English: Identifies business logic vulnerabilities via annotation-based sanitization in web applications.

中文: 通过 Web 应用中基于注释的清理识别业务逻辑漏洞。

7. Bullseye: Detecting Prototype Pollution in NPM Packages

Authors: Tariq Houis et al. (Concordia University)

English: Detects prototype pollution vulnerabilities in NPM packages with proof-of-concept exploits.

中文: 检测 NPM 包中的原型污染漏洞并提供概念验证利用。

四、电子邮件安全

8. CoordMail: SMTP Timeout Coordination for Amplification Attacks

Authors: Ruixuan Li et al. (Tsinghua, Zhongguancun Lab, Coremail)

English: Exploits SMTP timeout and command interaction to coordinate email middleware for convergence amplification attacks.

中文: 利用 SMTP 超时和命令交互协调电子邮件中间件进行收敛放大攻击。

五、Wi-Fi 与无线安全

9. AirSnitch: Breaking Client Isolation in Wi-Fi Networks

Authors: Xin’an Zhou et al. (UC Riverside, KU Leuven)

English: Demystifies and breaks Wi-Fi client isolation mechanisms, exposing vulnerabilities in public Wi-Fi security.

中文: 揭秘并打破 Wi-Fi 客户端隔离机制，暴露公共 Wi-Fi 安全漏洞。

六、网络测量与分析

10. Aliens Among Us: Observing Private IPs on the Public Internet

Authors: Radu Anghel et al. (TU Delft, ICANN, RIPE NCC, CAIDA)

English: Measurement study observing prevalence of private/reserved IP addresses on the public internet and their security implications.

中文: 测量研究观察公共互联网上私有/保留 IP 地址的流行度及其安全影响。

11. Enhancing Website Fingerprinting Attacks Against Traffic Drift

Authors: Xinhao Deng et al. (Tsinghua University, Ant Group)

English: Enhances website fingerprinting attacks to remain effective even when traffic patterns change over time.

中文: 增强网站指纹攻击以在流量模式随时间变化时保持有效。

12. Cease at the Ultimate Goodness: Efficient Website Fingerprinting Defense

Authors: Rong Wang et al. (Southeast University, UMass Lowell)

English: Efficient website fingerprinting defense via iterative mutual information minimization.

中文: 通过迭代互信息最小化实现高效的网站指纹防御。

七、网络隐私

13. Beyond RTT: Adversarially Robust Residential Proxy Detection

Authors: Temoor Ali et al. (Qatar Computing Research Institute, HBKU)

English: Adversarially robust two-tiered approach for residential proxy detection beyond simple RTT measurements.

中文: 超越简单 RTT 测量的对抗鲁棒双层住宅代理检测方法。

14. Connecting the Dots: Linking Private Data Across Messaging Apps

Authors: Junkyu Kang et al. (KAIST, University of Maryland)

English: Investigative study on linking private user data across different messaging applications.

中文: 跨不同消息应用链接私有用户数据的调查研究。

八、网络协议与标准

15. ACTS: Attestations of Contents in TLS Sessions

Authors: Pierpaolo Della Monica et al. (Sapienza University of Rome)

English: Enables attestations of contents in TLS sessions for verifiable secure communications.

中文: 为 TLS 会话中的内容启用证明以实现可验证的安全通信。

16. CTng: Secure Certificate and Revocation Transparency

Authors: Jie Kong et al. (University of Connecticut, et al.)

English: Improves certificate transparency with stronger security guarantees against malicious certificate authorities.

中文: 通过针对恶意证书颁发机构的更强安全保证改进证书透明度。

研究趋势

🌐 互联网基础设施

  1. RPKI 部署与安全: 全球 RPKI 部署增加,但底层基础设施威胁不容忽视
  2. DNS 仍是攻击向量: 缓存时序攻击等新型 DNS 利用方式持续出现
  3. 证书透明度进化: CT 日志安全性和可靠性持续改进

🔒 Web 安全

  1. 可解释性需求: 从黑盒检测转向可解释的威胁分析
  2. 业务逻辑漏洞: 超越传统注入攻击,关注业务逻辑缺陷
  3. 供应链安全: NPM、开源包中的漏洞成为重点
  4. DOM-XSS 持续: 客户端漏洞仍然是主要威胁

📧 电子邮件与通信

  1. 协议级攻击: SMTP 等老协议的新型利用方式
  2. 放大攻击: 利用协议机制实现流量放大

📶 无线网络

  1. 隔离机制失效: Wi-Fi 客户端隔离等安全边界被突破
  2. 隐私威胁: 网站指纹、流量分析等隐私攻击持续演进

💡 技术洞察

  • 基础设施脆弱性: 互联网关键基础设施(路由、DNS、PKI)仍存在系统性风险
  • 隐私与安全矛盾: 网站指纹等攻击凸显隐私保护的挑战
  • 协议老化问题: SMTP、BACnet 等老协议难以修复
  • 测量研究价值: 大规模测量研究揭示真实世界的安全态势

🏢 实际影响

企业:

  • Web 应用安全检测工具的可解释性需求
  • 供应链安全(NPM、开源包)管理

ISP/运营商:

  • RPKI 部署和基础设施安全
  • DNS 服务的抗攻击能力

用户隐私:

  • 网站指纹威胁浏览隐私
  • DNS 缓存攻击泄露行为

公共场所:

  • Wi-Fi 客户端隔离失效影响咖啡馆、机场等场所安全

其他视角:

  • AI 与机器学习安全视角
  • 系统安全视角
  • IoT 与嵌入式系统视角
  • 密码学与隐私视角

NDSS 2026 论文精选 - IoT 与嵌入式系统视角

从 IoT/嵌入式视角看 NDSS 2026: 涵盖固件安全、工业控制、自主系统、无线通信

Perspective: IoT & Embedded Systems | Focus: Firmware, ICS, Automotive, Wireless

一、固件安全与分析

1. FirmAgent: LLM-Assisted IoT Firmware Vulnerability Discovery

Authors: Jiangan Ji et al. (Information Engineering University, Tsinghua)

English: Combines fuzzing with LLM agents for enhanced IoT firmware vulnerability discovery through intelligent analysis.

中文: 结合模糊测试与 LLM 代理通过智能分析增强物联网固件漏洞发现。

2. FirmCross: Detecting Taint Vulnerabilities in C-Lua Hybrid Services

Authors: Runhao Liu et al. (NUDT, Fudan University)

English: Detects taint-style vulnerabilities in modern firmware using hybrid C-Lua web services, common in routers and IoT devices.

中文: 检测使用混合 C-Lua Web 服务的现代固件中的污点式漏洞，常见于路由器和物联网设备。

二、工业控制系统 (ICS) 安全

3. LLM-Driven Fuzzing for Detecting PLC Logic Bugs

Authors: Jiaxing Cheng et al. (IIE CAS, NJUST, Virginia Tech)

English: Leverages LLMs to drive PLC fuzzing, detecting critical logic instruction bugs in industrial control systems.

中文: 利用 LLM 驱动 PLC 模糊测试，检测工业控制系统中的关键逻辑指令错误。

4. Discovering Blind-Trust Vulnerabilities in PLC Binaries

Authors: Fangzhou Dong et al. (Arizona State University, New Mexico State)

English: Discovers blind-trust vulnerabilities in PLC binaries via state machine recovery, revealing critical ICS flaws.

中文: 通过状态机恢复发现 PLC 二进制文件中的盲信任漏洞，揭示关键 ICS 缺陷。

5. BACnet or “BADnet”? Security of Implicitly Reserved Fields

Authors: Qiguang Zhang et al. (Southeast University, Shandong University)

English: Exposes security vulnerabilities in BACnet protocol by exploiting implicitly reserved fields, threatening smart buildings.

中文: 通过利用隐式保留字段暴露 BACnet 协议中的安全漏洞，威胁智能建筑。

三、自主系统与机器人安全

6. ADGFUZZ: Assignment Dependency-Guided Fuzzing for Robotic Vehicles

Authors: Yuncheng Wang et al. (IIE CAS, Ant Group, Tsinghua)

English: Assignment dependency-guided fuzzing for robotic vehicles, significantly improving bug discovery in autonomous systems.

中文: 针对机器人车辆的赋值依赖引导模糊测试，显著提高自动系统中的错误发现率。

7. FlyTrap: Physical Distance-Pulling Attack on Camera Tracking

Authors: [Full author list from NDSS]

English: Demonstrates physical distance-pulling attacks against camera-based autonomous tracking systems via adversarial patterns.

中文: 通过对抗模式展示针对基于相机的自主跟踪系统的物理距离拉动攻击。

四、汽车安全

8. Automating Function-Level TARA for Automotive Security

Authors: Yuqiao Yang et al. (UESTC, GoGoByte Technology)

English: Automates function-level TARA (Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment) for automotive full-lifecycle security.

中文: 自动化汽车全生命周期安全的功能级威胁分析和风险评估 (TARA)。

五、无线通信安全

9. BLERP: BLE Re-Pairing Attacks and Defenses

Authors: Tommaso Sacchetti, Daniele Antonioli (EURECOM)

English: Identifies BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) re-pairing vulnerabilities and proposes effective defense mechanisms.

中文: 识别 BLE (低功耗蓝牙) 重新配对漏洞并提出有效的防御机制。

10. BSFuzzer: Context-Aware Semantic Fuzzing for BLE Logic Flaws

Authors: Ting Yang et al. (Xidian University, Kanazawa University, et al.)

English: Context-aware semantic fuzzing specifically designed to detect BLE logic flaws in IoT devices.

中文: 专门设计用于检测物联网设备中 BLE 逻辑缺陷的上下文感知语义模糊测试。

11. ANONYCALL: Enabling Native Private Calling in Mobile Networks

Authors: Hexuan Yu et al. (Virginia Tech, University of Kentucky, Georgia Tech)

English: Enables native private calling in mobile networks without requiring additional infrastructure or apps.

中文: 在移动网络中启用原生私密呼叫，无需额外基础设施或应用。

12. DualStrike: Accurate Real-time Keystroke Eavesdropping on Keyboards

Authors: Xiaomeng Chen et al. (Shanghai Jiao Tong University, UC Irvine)

English: Accurate, real-time eavesdropping and injection of keystrokes on commodity keyboards via electromagnetic emanations.

中文: 通过电磁辐射对商品键盘进行准确、实时的按键窃听和注入。

研究趋势

🎯 核心方向

  1. AI 赋能固件分析: LLM 应用于固件漏洞发现和逆向工程

Latest Cybersecurity Research Highlights - March 2026

This week brings exciting developments in cybersecurity research from arXiv, covering everything from automated scam detection to IoT security innovations and AI safety. Here’s a comprehensive summary of the most impactful papers published in late February and early March 2026.

1. Anansi: Scalable Job Scam Detection System

Paper: “Scalable Characterization of Message-Based Job Scams”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24223

Key Findings

Researchers have developed Anansi, the first scalable end-to-end measurement pipeline designed to systematically engage with and analyze job-based smishing scams. This automated system combines:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) for natural conversation with scammers
  • Automated browser agents to interact with scam infrastructure
  • Infrastructure fingerprinting tools for tracking campaigns

Impact

The system successfully:

  • Collected over 29,000 scam messages
  • Interacted with more than 1,900 scammers
  • Identified millions of dollars in cryptocurrency losses
  • Uncovered extensive reuse of message templates, domains, and crypto wallets

Significance

This research demonstrates the feasibility of automating scammer engagement and infrastructure analysis, offering a new methodological foundation for studying large-scale fraud ecosystems. The system revealed sophisticated deceptive techniques including domain fronting and brand impersonation.

2. SAILOR: Ultra-Lightweight RISC-V for IoT Security

Paper: “A Scalable and Energy-Efficient Ultra-Lightweight RISC-V for IoT Security”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24166

Innovation

SAILOR introduces a revolutionary modular RISC-V core family specifically designed for cryptographic applications in IoT devices. The design spans multiple data-path widths (1-, 2-, 4-, 8-, 16-, and 32-bit) to optimize for different use cases.

Performance Achievements

  • 13x improvement in performance and energy efficiency over state-of-the-art solutions
  • 59% reduction in area (hardware footprint)
  • Minimal overhead for integrating RISC-V cryptography extensions

Why It Matters

Most existing RISC-V cores for IoT prioritize either area or energy efficiency, often compromising security. SAILOR demonstrates that lightweight cryptographic features can be integrated without prohibitive overhead, making secure IoT devices more practical and affordable.

3. Jailbreak Foundry: Reproducible LLM Security Testing

Paper: “Jailbreak Foundry: From Papers to Runnable Attacks for Reproducible Benchmarking”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24009

The Problem

Jailbreak techniques for large language models evolve faster than benchmarks, making robustness estimates quickly outdated. Different papers use different datasets and judging protocols, making comparisons difficult.

The Solution

JAILBREAK FOUNDRY (JBF) uses a multi-agent workflow to automatically translate jailbreak research papers into executable attack modules. The system features:

  • JBF-LIB: Shared contracts and reusable utilities
  • JBF-FORGE: Multi-agent paper-to-module translation
  • JBF-EVAL: Standardized evaluation framework

Results

Across 30 reproduced attacks, JBF achieved:

  • High fidelity with mean deviation of only +0.26 percentage points from reported results
  • 82.5% mean reused-code ratio, reducing implementation effort
  • 50% reduction in attack-specific code compared to original repositories

This system enables standardized evaluation of attacks across 10 victim models, creating “living benchmarks” that keep pace with the rapidly shifting security landscape.

4. Feature Transferability in IoT Botnet Detection

Paper: “Exploring Robust Intrusion Detection: A Benchmark Study of Feature Transferability in IoT Botnet Attack Detection”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.23874
Conference: Accepted for ICISSP 2026

Research Focus

This comprehensive study evaluates how well intrusion detection features transfer across different IoT network environments—a critical challenge as network characteristics vary significantly across domains.

Methodology

The research evaluated three widely-used flow-based feature sets:

  • Argus
  • Zeek
  • CICFlowMeter

These were tested across four heterogeneous datasets representing various IoT and Industrial IoT conditions.

Key Findings

  • Models trained on one domain suffer significant performance degradation when applied to different target domains
  • Feature choice and classification algorithm critically impact transferability
  • Using SHapley Additive exPlanations (SHAP), researchers identified which features remain robust across domains

Practical Impact

The paper provides actionable guidelines for:

  • Feature engineering to improve robustness under domain variability
  • Algorithm selection for cross-domain resilience
  • Designing effective intrusion detection that works across diverse IoT environments

Emerging Trends

These papers collectively highlight several important trends in cybersecurity research:

  1. Automation is Key: From Anansi’s automated scam detection to JBF’s automated attack reproduction, automation is becoming central to keeping pace with evolving threats.

  2. Efficiency Meets Security: SAILOR demonstrates that security and efficiency are not mutually exclusive—critical for resource-constrained IoT devices.

  3. Cross-Domain Robustness: The IoT botnet study emphasizes that security solutions must work across diverse environments, not just in controlled lab settings.

  4. AI Safety Ecosystem: JBF’s living benchmark approach shows the community is building infrastructure for continuous security evaluation as LLMs evolve.

Conclusion

The cybersecurity landscape in March 2026 reflects a maturing field where researchers are not just finding vulnerabilities but building scalable systems to detect, prevent, and evaluate security threats across diverse domains—from cryptocurrency scams to IoT botnets to AI safety.

These advances represent significant steps toward more automated, efficient, and robust security solutions that can adapt to rapidly evolving threat landscapes.

IH&MMSec 由原先的 IH 和 ACM MM&Sec 两个 Workshops 合并而来。

For over a decade, two workshops have been shaping the landscape of research in multimedia security. On one hand, Information Hiding (aka. IH) was created in 1996 and focused on digital watermarking, steganography and steganalysis, anonymity and privacy, hard to intercept communications and covert/subliminal channels. On the other hand, the ACM Workshop on Multimedia and Security (aka. ACM MM&Sec) was initiated in 1998 and focused on data hiding, robust/perceptual hash, biometrics, video surveillance, and multimedia forensics. Key seminal works have been published in these two workshops and papers accepted for publication there attracted over 9,000 citations in total. Year after year, the two communities grew closer and the overlap between their respective scope got bigger. As a result, after 14 successful editions each, IH and ACM MMSec decided to merge in a single event in an attempt to establish synergies between the two communities while building on the reputation obtained over the years.
