编程工作流

它还内置了我已经写过的头脑风暴 -> 计划 -> 实施工作流。最大的变化是你不再需要运行命令或粘贴提示。如果 Claude 认为你正在尝试启动项目或任务，它应该默认在开始实施之前与你讨论计划。

头脑风暴完成后，如果你在 git 仓库中，它会自动为项目创建一个工作树并切换到该目录。这意味着你可以在同一个项目上启动并行任务而不会相互冲突。

然后它为你提供一个选择：

  • 上个月的流程（你会打开第二个 claude 会话，作为架构师和实施者的人类项目经理）
  • 本月的酷炫新流程，它逐个将任务分派给子代理实施，然后在继续之前对每个任务进行代码审查

无论哪种方式，Claude 都会实践 RED/GREEN TDD，编写一个失败的测试，实现足够的代码使该测试通过，然后继续。

在实施过程结束时，Claude 现在会提供创建 GitHub 拉取请求、将工作树合并回本地源分支或直接停止的选项。

但这些都不是有趣的部分。

有趣的部分

技能才是有趣的部分。而且你很快就会从……几乎所有人那里听到更多关于它们的消息。

技能是赋予你的代理超能力的东西。

它们第一次真正引起我的注意是几周前，当时 Anthropic 推出了改进的 Office 文档创建功能。当该功能推出时，我四处探索了一下——我让 Claude 告诉我它的新技能。它非常乐意透露。

之后，我开始到处看到看起来很像技能的东西。

几个星期五前我看到的一个非常酷的技术演示谈到了他们如何通过写出听起来很像 SKILL.md 文件的东西来赋予他们的自定义编程代理自我改进的能力。Sam Schillace 在这里写了关于那个演示的文章。

Sam 和 Brian Krabach 是 Microsoft Amplifier 背后的几个人之一，这是一个令人惊叹的集成开发框架，使用这种相同的模式——一个通过编写 markdown 文档和为自己编写工具来改进自己的编程代理。Amplifier 中有大量非常智能的东西，如果你对这个领域感兴趣，非常值得一看。

我教给 Superpowers 的第一个技能之一是如何创建技能。这意味着当我想做像为 Superpowers 添加 git 工作树工作流之类的事情时，只需描述我想要的工作流程……然后 Claude 将各个部分组合在一起，并为需要提示未来 Claude 使用工作树的现有技能添加了一些注释。

我还没有发布 Claude 和我构建的所有技能，因为其中一些有点深奥，而我玩过的一些来自告诉 Claude “这是我的编程书副本。请阅读这本书并提取在开始阅读之前对你不明显的可重用技能”，我还不确定我对此的知识产权方面的感觉如何。

停下来想想最后那个工作流。我认为这是所有这些如何工作的基础思想之一。你可以把一本书、一份文档或一个代码库交给模型，然后说”阅读这个。思考它。写下你学到的新东西。”它有时确实需要帮助模型通过特定的镜头（或一组镜头）来看待工作。但它非常强大。我一直在尝试让 Claude 一直记住这个想法，但我对输出还不太满意。

当 Claude 和我构建新技能时，我要求它做的一件事是在一组子代理上”测试”技能，以确保技能是可理解的、完整的，并且子代理会遵守它们。（Claude 现在将此视为技能的 TDD，并使用其 RED/GREEN TDD 技能作为技能创建技能的一部分。）

我们第一次玩这个游戏时，Claude 告诉我子代理得到了满分。经过一番追问，我发现 Claude 像在游戏节目中一样测验子代理。这没什么用。我要求切换到对代理施加压力的现实场景，以更好地模拟它们可能实际做什么。

结果比我希望的要好得多。再一次，我很高兴这项工作不受 IRB 审查。

1. MinBucket MPSI: Breaking the Max-Size Bottleneck

Authors: Binbin Tu, Boyudong Zhu, Yang Cao, Yu Chen (Shandong University)

Abstract

Multi-Party Private Set Intersection (Cardinality) protocol enables $T$ $(T > 2)$ parties, each holding a private set, to jointly compute the intersection (or its cardinality) without revealing any additional information to other parties. To date, all known MPSI (MPSI-Card) protocols require communication complexity that scales linearly with the size of the large set, fundamentally precluding their efficient deployment in real-world applications with heterogeneous input scales.

In this work, we present a new framework for MPSI based on newly proposed protocols: batched membership conditional randomness generation and joint private equality test. By instantiating this framework, we develop two MPSI protocols with communication complexities that are linear in the size of the small set and logarithmic in the size of the large set. One protocol offers security against an arbitrary number of colluding parties, while the other secures against $(T-2)$ colluding parties. Additionally, we develop a protocol called the joint permuted private equality test and propose the MPSI-Card framework. By instantiating this framework, we derive an MPSI-Card protocol with similar communication efficiency: linear in the small set and logarithmic in the large set, providing security against an arbitrary number of colluding parties.

We implement our protocols and conduct extensive experiments over both LAN and WAN networks. Experimental results demonstrate that our protocols achieve significantly better performance as the size difference between the sets or the number of participants holding the small set increases. For the setting, where $5$ parties holding large set (size $2^{20}$) and $5$ parties holding small set (size $2^{10}$) with a single thread and a $10$ Mbps bandwidth, our MPSI (MPSI-Card) protocol requires only $12.2$ ($12.2$) MB of communication and $129.86$ ($130.05$) seconds of runtime. Compared with the state-of-the-art MPSI by Wu et al. (USENIX Security 2024) and MPSI-Card by Gao et al. (PETS 2024), our protocol achieves a $157\times$ $(76\times)$ reduction in communication cost and a $12.7\times$ $(3.1\times)$ speedup in runtime.

摘要

多方隐私集合交集(基数)协议使得$T$($T > 2$)方各自持有私有集合，能够联合计算交集(或其基数)而不向其他方透露任何额外信息。迄今为止，所有已知的MPSI(MPSI-Card)协议都需要与大集合大小线性相关的通信复杂度，从根本上阻止了它们在具有异构输入规模的真实世界应用中的有效部署。

在这项工作中，我们基于新提出的协议提出了一个新的MPSI框架：批量成员条件随机性生成和联合私有相等性测试。通过实例化这个框架，我们开发了两个MPSI协议，其通信复杂度与小集合大小线性相关，与大集合大小对数相关。一个协议提供针对任意数量串通方的安全性，而另一个协议针对$(T-2)$串通方提供安全性。此外，我们开发了一个称为联合排列私有相等性测试的协议，并提出了MPSI-Card框架。通过实例化这个框架，我们推导出一个具有类似通信效率的MPSI-Card协议：与小集合线性相关，与大集合对数相关，提供针对任意数量串通方的安全性。

我们实现了我们的协议，并在LAN和WAN网络上进行了广泛的实验。实验结果表明，随着集合之间大小差异或持有小集合的参与者数量增加，我们的协议取得了显著更好的性能。对于5方持有大集合(大小$2^{20}$)和5方持有小集合(大小$2^{10}$)，使用单线程和10 Mbps带宽的设置，我们的MPSI(MPSI-Card)协议仅需要12.2(12.2)MB通信和129.86(130.05)秒运行时间。与Wu等人(USENIX Security 2024)的最先进MPSI和Gao等人(PETS 2024)的MPSI-Card相比，我们的协议在通信成本方面实现了157倍(76倍)的降低，在运行时间方面实现了12.7倍(3.1倍)的加速。

2. Faster Than Ever: Lightweight Private Set Intersection

Authors: Guowei Ling, Peng Tang, Liyao Xiang, Weidong Qiu (Shanghai Jiao Tong), Jinyong Shan (Beijing Smartchip Microelectronics)

Abstract

In this work, we present a new lightweight two-party Private Set Intersection (PSI) paradigm in both the semi-honest and malicious models. It requires only a small number of base OTs and a single Oblivious Key-Value Stores (OKVS) encoding and decoding. All computations (except for the base OTs) can be implemented using SIMD-accelerated symmetric cryptographic instructions and efficient bitwise operations. Furthermore, we extend the proposed PSI protocol to circuit PSI and, subsequently, to several PSI variants, including PSI-cardinality, PSI-sum, and Private Join and Compute (PJC).

All proposed protocols are evaluated under both LAN and WAN settings, with performance compared against existing works. Experimental results show that the proposed PSI outperforms the most efficient VOLE-based PSI by approximately 40% in runtime, while consistently incurring lower communication overhead under the same settings. For circuit PSI, it is up to $3.7\times$ faster and reduces communication by a factor of $1.5$ compared to VOLE-based circuit PSI constructions. In the cases of PSI-cardinality and PSI-sum, it achieves speedups of up to $12.4\times$ and $10\times$, respectively, while incurring only moderate communication overhead. For PJC, the proposed protocol outperforms prior work by $762\times$ in runtime and achieves a $3.2\times$ reduction in communication, maintaining high efficiency even under a low-bandwidth condition.

摘要

在这项工作中，我们在半诚实和恶意模型中提出了一个新的轻量级双方隐私集合交集(PSI)范式。它仅需要少量基础OT和单个遗忘键值存储(OKVS)编码和解码。所有计算(除了基础OT)都可以使用SIMD加速的对称加密指令和高效的位运算来实现。此外，我们将提出的PSI协议扩展到电路PSI，随后扩展到几个PSI变体，包括PSI基数、PSI求和和私有连接与计算(PJC)。

所有提出的协议都在LAN和WAN设置下进行评估，性能与现有工作进行比较。实验结果表明，提出的PSI在运行时间上优于最高效的基于VOLE的PSI约40%，同时在相同设置下始终产生较低的通信开销。对于电路PSI，与基于VOLE的电路PSI构造相比，它快达3.7倍，并将通信减少了1.5倍。在PSI基数和PSI求和的情况下，它分别实现了高达12.4倍和10倍的加速，同时仅产生适度的通信开销。对于PJC，提出的协议在运行时间上优于先前工作762倍，并实现了3.2倍的通信减少，即使在低带宽条件下也能保持高效率。

3. cwPSU: Efficient Unbalanced Private Set Union

Authors: Qingwen Li (Xidian University), Song Bian (Beihang University), Hui Li (Xidian University)

Abstract

Private Set Union (PSU) allows two parties to compute the union of their private sets without revealing any additional information. While several PSU protocols have been proposed for the unbalanced setting, these constructions still suffer from substantial communication overhead as the size of the larger set increases. Moreover, their reliance on multiple invocations of oblivious pseudo-random functions results in increased communication rounds, which becomes a practical bottleneck.

In this work, we present cwPSU, a novel unbalanced PSU protocol built upon constant-weight codes and leveled fully homomorphic encryption. To prevent leakage, we introduce a new technique called Batched Ciphertext Shuffle, which enables secure reordering of packed ciphertexts. Additionally, we propose an optimized arithmetic constant-weight equality operator, which reduces the number of non-scalar multiplications to just one-third of those required by the naïve approach. The communication complexity of our protocol scales linearly with the size of the smaller set and remains independent of the larger set. Notably, cwPSU requires only a single round of online communication.

Experimental results demonstrate that our cwPSU outperforms the state-of-the-art protocol in various network conditions, achieving a $5.1$–$32.4\times$ reduction in communication and a $3.1$–$13.3\times$ speedup in runtime.

摘要

隐私集合并集(PSU)允许双方计算其私有集合的并集而不透露任何额外信息。虽然已经为不平衡设置提出了几个PSU协议，但随着较大集合大小的增加，这些构造仍然遭受大量通信开销的困扰。此外，它们依赖于遗忘伪随机函数的多次调用导致通信轮次增加，这成为一个实际瓶颈。

在这项工作中，我们提出了cwPSU，这是一个基于常重码和分级完全同态加密构建的新型不平衡PSU协议。为了防止泄漏，我们引入了一种称为批量密文洗牌的新技术，它能够安全地重新排序打包的密文。此外，我们提出了一个优化的算术常重相等性运算符，它将非标量乘法的数量减少到朴素方法所需数量的仅三分之一。我们协议的通信复杂度与较小集合的大小线性相关，并且与较大集合无关。值得注意的是，cwPSU仅需要一轮在线通信。

实验结果表明，我们的cwPSU在各种网络条件下优于最先进的协议，实现了5.1至32.4倍的通信减少和3.1至13.3倍的运行时间加速。

4. BKPIR: Keyword PIR for Private Boolean Retrieval

Authors: Jie Song, Zhen Xu, Yan Zhang (IIE, CAS), Pengwei Zhan (Sangfor Technologies), Mingxuan Li (PPSUC), Shuai Ma (Beihang), Ru Xie (IIE, CAS)

Abstract

Keyword Private Information Retrieval (Keyword PIR) enables users to retrieve data associated with specific keywords from a database while keeping their queries private. However, existing Keyword PIR schemes struggle to support the boolean retrieval model, which is essential for practical applications that require logical combinations of terms.

This paper proposes a novel keyword PIR scheme leveraging advancements in homomorphic equality operations. It enables privacy-preserving retrieval over databases with many-to-many keyword-value mappings while supporting boolean operators for expressive search logic. Importantly, this extension preserves the core security guarantees of classical PIR. To the best of our knowledge, this is the first work to integrate keyword PIR with the boolean retrieval model.

Experimental evaluation shows that our scheme achieves a communication cost reduction proportional to the total number of values in the many-to-many keyword-value database, along with aggregate query processing performance gains that scale linearly with the number of values. These improvements enhance its feasibility for real-world applications such as privacy-preserving web search and patent retrieval.

摘要

我们在已知参与模型中研究具有拜占庭故障和睡眠副本的共识，其中诚实副本可能不可预测地入睡，并且副本知道清醒诚实副本的最小数量。我们的主要贡献是在这种混合故障模型中提供共识的细粒度处理。

首先，我们提出了一个同步原子广播协议，具有$5\Delta+2\delta$的预期延迟和$2\Delta+2\delta$的最佳情况延迟，其中$\Delta$是网络延迟的界限，$\delta$是实际网络延迟。其次，在部分同步网络中($\Delta$的值未知)，我们表明可以使传统的拜占庭容错(BFT)协议容忍睡眠副本，但必须做出稳定存储假设(其中副本需要在稳定存储中存储中间共识参数)。

最后，在部分同步网络中但不假设稳定存储，我们展示了副本总数$n$、拜占庭副本最大数量$f$和同时睡眠副本最大数量$s$之间关系的几个界限。使用这些界限，我们将HotStuff(PODC’19)转换为一个容忍睡眠副本而不牺牲性能的协议。

14. Cross-Consensus Reliable Broadcast and Applications

Authors: Yue Huang, Xin Wang (Tsinghua), Haibin Zhang (Yangtze Delta Region Institute of Tsinghua), Sisi Duan (Tsinghua)

Abstract

Conventional Byzantine fault-tolerant consensus protocols focus on the workflow within a group of nodes. In recent years, many applications of consensus involve communication across groups. Examples include communication between replicated state machine across different infrastructures, sharding-based protocols where nodes from different shards communicate with each other, and cross-chain bridges. Unfortunately, little effort has been made to model the properties for communication across groups.

In this work, we propose a new primitive called cross-consensus reliable broadcast (XRBC). The XRBC primitive models the security properties of communication between two groups, where at least one group executes a consensus protocol. We provide three constructions of XRBC under different assumptions and present three different applications for our XRBC protocols: a cross-shard coordination protocol via a case study of Reticulum (NDSS 2024), a protocol for cross-shard transactions via a case study of Chainspace (NDSS 2018), and a solution for cross-chain bridge.

摘要

传统的拜占庭容错共识协议专注于一组节点内的工作流程。近年来，许多共识应用涉及跨组通信。示例包括不同基础设施之间的复制状态机之间的通信、基于分片的协议(其中来自不同分片的节点彼此通信)以及跨链桥。不幸的是，很少努力对跨组通信的属性进行建模。

在这项工作中，我们提出了一个称为**跨共识可靠广播(XRBC)**的新原语。XRBC原语对两个组之间通信的安全属性进行建模，其中至少一个组执行共识协议。我们在不同假设下提供了三种XRBC构造，并为我们的XRBC协议提出了三个不同的应用：通过Reticulum(NDSS 2024)案例研究的跨分片协调协议、通过Chainspace(NDSS 2018)案例研究的跨分片交易协议以及跨链桥的解决方案。

我们的评估结果表明，我们的协议非常高效并使不同的应用受益。例如，在我们对Reticulum的案例研究中，我们的方法实现了比原始方法低61.16%的延迟。

15. Distributed Broadcast Encryption for Private Blockchains

Authors: Angelo De Caro, Kaoutar Elkhiyaoui (IBM Research Zurich), Sandeep Nishad, Sikhar Patranabis, Venkatraman Ramakrishna (IBM Research India)

Abstract

Interoperation across distributed ledger technology (DLT) networks hinges upon the secure transmission of ledger state from one network to another. This is especially challenging for private networks whose ledger access is limited to enrolled members. Existing approaches rely on a trusted centralized proxy that receives encrypted ledger state of a network, decrypts it, and sends it to members of another network. Though effective, this approach goes against the founding principle of DLT, namely avoiding single points of failure (or single sources of trust).

In this paper, we leverage fully-distributed broadcast encryption (FDBE in short) to build a fully decentralized protocol for confidential information-sharing across private networks. Compared to traditional broadcast encryption (BE), FDBE is characterized by distributed setup and key generation, where mutually distrusting parties agree on a BE’s public key without a trusted setup, and securely derive their decryption keys. Given any FDBE, two private networks can securely share information as follows: a sender in one network uses the other network’s FDBE public key to encrypt a message for its members. The resulting construction is secure in the simplified universal composability (UC) framework.

To further demonstrate the practicality of our approach, we present the first instantiation of an FDBE that enjoys constant-sized decryption keys and ciphertexts, and evaluate the resulting performances through a reference implementation that considers two private Hyperledger Fabric networks within the Hyperledger Cacti interoperation framework.

摘要

分布式账本技术(DLT)网络之间的互操作依赖于从一个网络到另一个网络的账本状态的安全传输。这对于账本访问仅限于注册成员的私有网络尤其具有挑战性。现有方法依赖于可信的集中式代理，该代理接收网络的加密账本状态，解密它，并将其发送给另一个网络的成员。虽然有效，但这种方法违背了DLT的创始原则，即避免单点故障(或单一信任来源)。

在本文中，我们利用完全分布式广播加密(简称FDBE)来构建一个完全去中心化的协议，用于私有网络之间的机密信息共享。与传统广播加密(BE)相比，FDBE的特点是分布式设置和密钥生成，其中相互不信任的各方在没有可信设置的情况下就BE的公钥达成一致，并安全地派生其解密密钥。给定任何FDBE，两个私有网络可以安全地共享信息如下：一个网络中的发送者使用另一个网络的FDBE公钥为其成员加密消息。生成的构造在简化的通用可组合性(UC)框架中是安全的。

为了进一步证明我们方法的实用性，我们提出了第一个享有常量大小解密密钥和密文的FDBE实例化，并通过参考实现评估生成的性能，该实现考虑了Hyperledger Cacti互操作框架内的两个私有Hyperledger Fabric网络。

16. Abuse Resistant Traceability for Encrypted Messaging

Authors: Zhongming Wang, Tao Xiang, Xiaoguo Li, Biwen Chen, Ze Jiang, Chuan Ma (Chongqing University), Guomin Yang (SMU), Jiacheng Wang (NTU), Robert H. Deng (SMU)

Abstract

Encrypted messaging systems provide end-to-end security for users but obstruct content moderation, making it difficult to combat online abuses. Traceability offers a promising solution by enabling platforms to identify the originator or spreader of messages, yet this capability can be abused for mass surveillance of innocent messages. To mitigate this risk, existing approaches restrict traceability to problematic messages that are reported by multiple users or are on a predefined blocklist. However, these solutions either over-trust a specific entity (e.g., the party defining the blocklist) or rely on the unrealistic assumption of non-collusion between servers run by a single platform.

In this paper, we propose an abuse-resistant source tracing scheme that distributes traceability across distinct real-world entities. Specifically, we formally define its syntax and prove its security properties. Our scheme realizes two essential principles: minimal trust, which ensures that traceability cannot be abused as long as a single participant involved in tracing is honest, even if all others collude; and minimal information disclosure, which prevents participants from acquiring any information (e.g., communication parties’ identities) unnecessary for tracing.

We implemented our scheme using techniques deployed by Signal, and our evaluation shows it offers comparable performance to state-of-the-art schemes that are vulnerable to abuse.

摘要

加密消息系统为用户提供端到端安全性，但阻碍了内容审核，使得打击在线滥用变得困难。可追溯性通过使平台能够识别消息的发起者或传播者提供了一个有前途的解决方案，但这种能力可能被滥用于对无辜消息的大规模监视。为了减轻这种风险，现有方法将可追溯性限制在由多个用户报告或在预定义黑名单上的有问题的消息。然而，这些解决方案要么过度信任特定实体(例如定义黑名单的一方)，要么依赖于单个平台运行的服务器之间不串通的不切实际假设。

在本文中，我们提出了一个抗滥用的源追踪方案，它将可追溯性分布在不同的真实世界实体之间。具体而言，我们正式定义了其语法并证明了其安全属性。我们的方案实现了两个基本原则：最小信任，确保只要参与追踪的单个参与者诚实，即使所有其他人串通，可追溯性也不能被滥用；以及最小信息披露，防止参与者获取任何追踪不必要的信息(例如通信方的身份)。

我们使用Signal部署的技术实现了我们的方案，我们的评估表明它提供了与容易受到滥用的最先进方案相当的性能。

17. CTng: Secure Certificate and Revocation Transparency

Authors: Jie Kong, Damon James (UConn), Hemi Leibowitz (College of Management, Israel), Ewa Syta (Trinity College), Amir Herzberg (UConn)

Abstract

We present CTng, an evolutionary and practical PKI design that efficiently addresses multiple key challenges faced by deployed PKI systems. CTng ensures strong security properties, including guaranteed transparency of certificates and guaranteed, unequivocal revocation, achieved under NTTP-security, i.e., without requiring trust in any single CA, logger, or relying party. These guarantees hold even in the presence of arbitrary corruptions of these entities, assuming only a known bound (f) of corrupt monitors (e.g., f=8), with minimal performance impact.

CTng also enables offline certificate validation and preserves relying-party privacy, while providing scalable and efficient distribution of revocation updates. These properties significantly improve upon current PKI designs. In particular, while Certificate Transparency (CT) aims to eliminate single points of trust, the existing specification still assumes benign loggers. Addressing this through log redundancy is possible, but rather inefficient, limiting deployed configurations to f ≤ 2.

We present a security analysis and an evaluation of our open-source CTng prototype, showing that it is efficient and scalable under realistic deployment conditions.

摘要

我们提出了CTng，这是一个演化的和实用的PKI设计，有效地解决了部署的PKI系统面临的多个关键挑战。CTng确保强大的安全属性，包括保证证书的透明度和保证的、明确的撤销，在NTTP安全下实现，即不需要信任任何单个CA、日志记录器或依赖方。即使在这些实体的任意损坏存在的情况下，这些保证也成立，仅假设已知的损坏监视器界限(f)(例如f=8)，具有最小的性能影响。

CTng还支持离线证书验证并保护依赖方隐私，同时提供可扩展且高效的撤销更新分发。这些属性显著改进了当前的PKI设计。特别是，虽然证书透明度(CT)旨在消除单点信任，但现有规范仍然假设良性日志记录器。通过日志冗余解决这个问题是可能的，但相当低效，将部署配置限制为f≤2。

我们提出了安全分析和我们开源CTng原型的评估，表明它在现实部署条件下是高效和可扩展的。

18. ANStega: Breaking the Generative Steganography Trilemma

Authors: Yaofei Wang, Weilong Pang (Hefei UT), Kejiang Chen, Jinyang Ding, Weiming Zhang, Nenghai Yu (USTC), Donghui Hu (Hefei UT)

Abstract

Generative steganography shows immense promise for covert communication, yet existing methods are often constrained by a trilemma of capacity, efficiency, and security. Methods based on Huffman Coding (HC) suffer from poor efficiency and security, while those based on Arithmetic Coding (AC), despite achieving optimal capacity, also pose security risks. Although recent provably secure methods have addressed the security issue, they often do so at the cost of elevated embedding complexity or diminished capacity—failing to match the high capacity exhibited by AC-based methods.

To address this trilemma, we adapt Asymmetric Numeral Systems (ANS) for steganography. Our core insight is to repurpose the ANS state machine, using its decoding function for embedding and its encoding function for extraction. To translate this concept into a practical system, we introduce several key innovations. First, we incorporate a streaming architecture with state renormalization to enable the stable embedding of arbitrarily long messages. Second, we employ direct floating-point arithmetic, avoiding costly probability-to-frequency conversions to reduce complexity and precision loss.

More critically, we introduce an innovative cryptographic mask mechanism that ensures the sampling process is driven by a cryptographically secure pseudo-random number generator, thereby achieving provable security. Finally, by optimizing core computations into highly efficient bitwise shift operations, ANStega achieves exceptional embedding and extraction speeds.

Experimental results validate that ANStega simultaneously achieves optimal embedding capacity, optimal efficiency (embedding complexity with $O(1)$) and optimal security, successfully resolving the long-standing trilemma in generative steganography.

摘要

生成式隐写术显示出巨大的隐蔽通信前景，但现有方法通常受到容量、效率和安全性三难困境的限制。基于霍夫曼编码(HC)的方法遭受效率和安全性差的困扰，而基于算术编码(AC)的方法尽管实现了最佳容量，但也带来了安全风险。虽然最近可证明安全的方法已经解决了安全问题，但它们通常以提高嵌入复杂性或降低容量为代价——未能匹配基于AC的方法展示的高容量。

为了解决这个三难困境，我们将非对称数字系统(ANS)应用于隐写术。我们的核心洞察力是重新利用ANS状态机，使用其解码函数进行嵌入，使用其编码函数进行提取。为了将这个概念转化为实用系统，我们引入了几个关键创新。首先，我们结合了具有状态重新规范化的流式架构，以实现任意长消息的稳定嵌入。其次，我们采用直接浮点算术，避免代价高昂的概率到频率转换以减少复杂性和精度损失。

更关键的是，我们引入了一个创新的加密掩码机制，确保采样过程由加密安全的伪随机数生成器驱动，从而实现可证明的安全性。最后，通过将核心计算优化为高效的位移操作，ANStega实现了卓越的嵌入和提取速度。

实验结果验证了ANStega同时实现了最佳嵌入容量、最佳效率(嵌入复杂度为$O(1)$)和最佳安全性，成功解决了生成式隐写术中长期存在的三难困境。

1. Crack in the Armor: RPKI Publication Point Reachability

Authors: Yunhao Liu, Jessie Hui Wang, Yangyang Wang, Jilong Wang (Tsinghua & Zhongguancun Lab), Yuedong Xu (Fudan), Zongpeng Li (Tsinghua)

Abstract

The effectiveness of the RPKI in preventing BGP prefix hijacking relies not only on the presence of valid ROAs but also on the successful retrieval of ROAs from publication points (PPs) by relying parties (RPs). Guaranteeing the integrity of data and uninterrupted connectivity during this retrieval process necessitates the proper implementation of security measures in the underlying infrastructure, i.e., the DNS and routing infrastructures.

In this paper, we collect information on the specific DNS and routing infrastructures used during the information retrieval process and analyze the infrastructure threats to the reachability of RPKI PPs. Regarding the DNS infrastructure, we report that 31 PPs (48.4%) are susceptible to DNS spoofing attacks and pinpoint the reasons for the appearance of DNSSEC-unprotected zones, such as CNAME redirections to unprotected zones and NS delegations to third-party insecure DNS servers. Regarding the routing infrastructure for communicating with nameservers, our analysis shows that a significant 55 PPs (85.9%) have at least one ROA-unprotected nameserver on their resolution paths, and highlights that the absence of ROA registration for gTLD nameservers accounts for vulnerabilities in 44 of these 55 PPs. Regarding the routing infrastructure for RP-PP communications, we report that 5 PPs fail to register ROAs for the IP addresses of their PP servers. Simulations of routing hijack attacks show that, in the case of the most vulnerable PP, up to 65% to 83% of ASes may experience a loss of connectivity to the PP.

Furthermore, we investigate the deterministic and probabilistic dependencies among publication points and uncover a critical issue: some RIR-operated PPs rely on less secure lower-level PPs, which can significantly amplify the impact of vulnerabilities within insecure PPs, potentially leading to cascading failures.

摘要

RPKI在防止BGP前缀劫持方面的有效性不仅依赖于有效ROA的存在，还依赖于依赖方(RP)从发布点(PP)成功检索ROA。在此检索过程中保证数据完整性和不间断连接需要在底层基础设施(即DNS和路由基础设施)中正确实施安全措施。

在本文中，我们收集有关信息检索过程中使用的特定DNS和路由基础设施的信息，并分析对RPKI PP可达性的基础设施威胁。关于DNS基础设施，我们报告31个PP(48.4%)易受DNS欺骗攻击，并指出DNSSEC未保护区域出现的原因，例如CNAME重定向到未保护区域和NS委托给第三方不安全的DNS服务器。关于与名称服务器通信的路由基础设施，我们的分析显示，显著的55个PP(85.9%)在其解析路径上至少有一个未受ROA保护的名称服务器，并强调gTLD名称服务器缺少ROA注册导致这55个PP中44个存在漏洞。关于RP-PP通信的路由基础设施，我们报告5个PP未能为其PP服务器的IP地址注册ROA。路由劫持攻击的模拟显示，在最脆弱的PP情况下，高达65%至83%的AS可能会经历与PP的连接丢失。

此外，我们调查发布点之间的确定性和概率依赖关系，揭示了一个关键问题：一些RIR运营的PP依赖于不太安全的较低级别PP，这可能会显著放大不安全PP内漏洞的影响，可能导致级联故障。

2. Demystifying RPKI-Invalid Prefixes

Authors: Weitong Li, Tijay Chung (Virginia Tech), Tao Wan (CableLabs)

Abstract

The Resource Public Key Infrastructure (RPKI) enhances Internet routing security by utilizing Route Origin Authorization (ROA) objects to link IP prefixes with their rightful origin ASNs. Despite the rapid deployment of RPKI—over 51.3% of Internet routes now covered by ROAs, there are still 6,802 RPKI-invalid prefixes as of today. This work provides the first comprehensive study to understand and classify the hidden causes of RPKI-invalid prefixes, revealing that ROA misconfigurations often occur during IP leasing and IP transit services. We identify scenarios explaining these misconfigurations and attribute 96.9% of the RPKI-invalid prefixes to such misconfigurations.

We further show their cascading impacts on the data plane, noting that while most prefixes exhibit negligible effects, 3.1% result in full connectivity loss and 7.1% degrade routing by adding latency and extra hop counts—and, in some cases, bypassing intended security mechanisms; additionally, we find that such misconfigurations have been triggering false alarms in hijack detection systems. To validate our findings, we build a ground-truth dataset of 294 misconfigured prefixes through direct engagement with 174 network operators. We also interviewed 16 large ISPs and major leasing brokers about their ROA management practices, and we propose suggestions to avert ROA misconfigurations.

Taken together, this study not only fills gaps left by previous research but also offers actionable recommendations to network operators for improving ROA management and minimizing the occurrence of RPKI-invalid announcements.

摘要

资源公钥基础设施(RPKI)通过利用路由起源授权(ROA)对象将IP前缀与其合法起源ASN链接来增强互联网路由安全性。尽管RPKI部署迅速——目前超过51.3%的互联网路由被ROA覆盖，但截至今天仍有6,802个RPKI无效前缀。这项工作提供了第一个全面的研究，以理解和分类RPKI无效前缀的隐藏原因，揭示ROA错误配置经常发生在IP租赁和IP传输服务期间。我们识别解释这些错误配置的场景，并将96.9%的RPKI无效前缀归因于此类错误配置。

我们进一步展示了它们对数据平面的级联影响，注意到虽然大多数前缀表现出微不足道的影响，但3.1%导致完全连接丢失，7.1%通过增加延迟和额外跳数降低路由性能——在某些情况下，绕过预期的安全机制；此外，我们发现此类错误配置一直在劫持检测系统中触发误报。为了验证我们的发现，我们通过与174个网络运营商的直接接触构建了294个错误配置前缀的真实数据集。我们还采访了16个大型ISP和主要租赁经纪人关于他们的ROA管理实践，并提出避免ROA错误配置的建议。

总的来说，这项研究不仅填补了先前研究留下的空白，还为网络运营商改进ROA管理和最小化RPKI无效公告的发生提供了可操作的建议。

3. Aliens Among Us: Bogon Traffic on the Public Internet

Authors: Radu Anghel, Yury Zhauniarovich (TU Delft), Carlos Gañán (ICANN), Qasim Lone (RIPE NCC), Matthew Luckie (CAIDA)

Abstract

Spoofed traffic remains a major network hygiene concern, as it enables Distributed Denial-of-Service (DDoS) attacks by obscuring attack origins and hindering forensic analysis. 基于机器学习(ML)的恶意流量检测是一种有前途的安全范式。它通过识别各种高级攻击优于基于规则的传统检测。然而，这些ML模型的鲁棒性在很大程度上未被探索，从而允许攻击者制作逃避检测的对抗性流量示例。现有的逃避攻击通常依赖于过度限制性条件(例如加密协议、Tor或专门设置)，或需要目标的详细先验知识(例如训练数据和模型参数)，这在现实的黑盒场景中是不切实际的。硬标签黑盒逃避攻击的可行性(即适用于不同任务和协议而无需内部目标洞察)因此仍然是一个开放的挑战。

为此，我们开发了NetMasquerade，它利用强化学习(RL)操纵攻击流以模仿良性流量并逃避检测。具体而言，我们建立了一个名为Traffic-BERT的定制预训练模型，利用网络专用分词器和注意力机制提取多样化的良性流量模式。随后，我们将Traffic-BERT集成到RL框架中，允许NetMasquerade基于良性流量模式以最小修改有效地操纵恶意数据包序列。

实验结果表明，NetMasquerade使暴力和隐蔽攻击能够在80个攻击场景下逃避6种现有检测方法，实现超过96.65%的攻击成功率。值得注意的是，它可以逃避那些在经验上或可证明地对现有逃避攻击具有鲁棒性的方法。最后，NetMasquerade实现低延迟对抗性流量生成，证明了其在现实世界场景中的实用性。

11. Proteus: Adaptive Website Fingerprinting Attack

Authors: Xinhao Deng (Tsinghua & Ant), Yixiang Zhang, Qi Li, Zhuotao Liu, Ke Xu (Tsinghua & Zhongguancun Lab), Yabo Wang (Tsinghua)

Abstract

Anonymous communication systems, e.g., Tor, are vulnerable to various website fingerprinting (WF) attacks, which analyze network traffic patterns to compromise user privacy. In particular, sophisticated attacks employ deep learning (DL) models to identify distinctive traffic patterns associated with specific websites, allowing the adversary to determine which websites users have visited. However, these attacks are not designed to handle traffic drift, such as changes in website content and network conditions. Since traffic drift is common in real-world, the effectiveness of these attacks diminishes significantly in real-world deployment.

To address this limitation, we develop Proteus, the first adaptive WF attack framework to effectively mitigate the impact of traffic drift while maintaining robust performance in real-world scenarios. The key design rationale of Proteus is to continuously fine-tune the WF model using only drifted traffic without ground-truth labels collected while deploying the model, enabling the model to adapt to complex traffic drift in near real time. Specifically, Proteus aligns the feature distributions of original and drifted traffic by minimizing the maximum mean discrepancy and thus enhances model confidence by optimizing the entropy distribution of its predictions. Furthermore, it utilizes a Gaussian mixture model to obtain reliable pseudo labels, which are subsequently used in supervised fine-tuning to further enhance its robustness against drifted traffic.

Notably, Proteus can be seamlessly integrated with existing DL-based WF attacks to enhance their resilience to traffic drift. We evaluate Proteus on large-scale datasets containing over 350,000 real-world Tor browsing traces across six traffic drift scenarios. The results demonstrate that Proteus achieves an average 94.24% relative improvement in F1-score over eight state-of-the-art WF attacks for identifying drifted traffic.

摘要

匿名通信系统(例如Tor)容易受到各种网站指纹(WF)攻击，这些攻击分析网络流量模式以损害用户隐私。特别是，复杂的攻击采用深度学习(DL)模型来识别与特定网站相关的独特流量模式，允许对手确定用户访问了哪些网站。然而，这些攻击不是为处理流量漂移而设计的，例如网站内容和网络条件的变化。由于流量漂移在现实世界中很常见，这些攻击的有效性在现实世界部署中显著降低。

为了解决这一限制，我们开发了Proteus，这是第一个自适应WF攻击框架，在现实世界场景中有效缓解流量漂移的影响同时保持强大的性能。Proteus的关键设计原理是仅使用部署模型时收集的没有真实标签的漂移流量持续微调WF模型，使模型能够近实时适应复杂的流量漂移。具体而言，Proteus通过最小化最大平均差异来对齐原始和漂移流量的特征分布，从而通过优化其预测的熵分布来增强模型置信度。此外，它利用高斯混合模型获得可靠的伪标签，随后用于监督微调以进一步增强其对漂移流量的鲁棒性。

值得注意的是，Proteus可以无缝集成现有的基于DL的WF攻击以增强其对流量漂移的弹性。我们在包含超过350,000个真实世界Tor浏览痕迹的大规模数据集上评估Proteus，涵盖六个流量漂移场景。结果表明，Proteus在识别漂移流量方面在八个最先进的WF攻击上平均实现94.24%的F1分数相对改进。

12. FRUGAL: Efficient Website Fingerprinting Defense

Authors: Rong Wang, Zhen Ling, Guangchi Liu, Shaofeng Li, Junzhou Luo (Southeast University), Xinwen Fu (UMass Lowell)

Abstract

In response to growing online privacy threats, the Tor network offers essential protection against surveillance by routing traffic through a decentralized, encrypted infrastructure. However, Website Fingerprinting Attacks (WFA) present a formidable challenge to Tor’s anonymity. This paper introduces FRUGAL, a traffic obfuscation method that leverages the mutual information (MI) reduction between website traffic and labels as an optimization goal, advancing a novel perspective for Website Fingerprinting Defense (WFD).

By strategically injecting dummy packets at positions within website traffic that contribute most to cumulative MI reduction, FRUGAL achieves notable performance compared to state-of-the-art (SOTA) defense mechanisms. It effectively reduces attack success rates (ASR) across diverse attack models while maintaining minimal bandwidth overhead (BWO) and mitigating the impact of adversarial training. Extensive experiments validate the efficacy of FRUGAL across a comprehensive set of scenarios, including closed-world, open-world, and real-world simulation settings.

For example, in the closed-world setting, FRUGAL reduces the ASR of the DF model to 2.68% with a 30% BWO, substantially outperforming previous SOTA defenses, such as Palette (11.54% with 87% BWO). When the BWO of FRUGAL is increased to a comparable level of 80%, the ASR further drops below 1%, demonstrating significant resilience by remaining low at 9.42% even after adversarial training, compared to 20.27% for Palette. This work not only introduces a fresh perspective on WFD research but also establishes FRUGAL as a robust and universal defense framework against WFA.

摘要

为应对日益增长的在线隐私威胁，Tor网络通过分散的加密基础设施路由流量，提供对监视的基本保护。然而，网站指纹攻击(WFA)对Tor的匿名性提出了严峻挑战。本文介绍了FRUGAL，这是一种流量混淆方法，利用网站流量和标签之间的互信息(MI)减少作为优化目标，为网站指纹防御(WFD)推进了一个新颖的视角。

通过在网站流量中对累积MI减少贡献最大的位置策略性地注入虚拟数据包，FRUGAL与最先进(SOTA)的防御机制相比取得了显著的性能。它有效地降低了不同攻击模型的攻击成功率(ASR)，同时保持最小的带宽开销(BWO)并减轻对抗训练的影响。广泛的实验验证了FRUGAL在包括封闭世界、开放世界和真实世界模拟设置在内的综合场景集中的有效性。

例如，在封闭世界设置中，FRUGAL将DF模型的ASR降低到2.68%，BWO为30%，大大优于以前的SOTA防御，如Palette(11.54%，BWO 87%)。当FRUGAL的BWO增加到可比水平的80%时，ASR进一步下降到1%以下，通过在对抗训练后仍保持低至9.42%来展示显著的弹性，相比之下Palette为20.27%。这项工作不仅为WFD研究引入了新的视角，而且将FRUGAL确立为针对WFA的强大且通用的防御框架。

13. CoLD: Collaborative Label Denoising for Network IDS

Authors: Shuo Yang, Jinze Li, Jinfeng Xu, Edith C. H. Ngai (HKU), Xinran Zheng (UCL)

Abstract

Label noise presents a significant challenge in network intrusion detection, leading to erroneous classifications and decreased detection accuracy. Existing methods for handling noisy labels often lack deep insight into network traffic and blindly reconstruct the label distribution to filter samples with noisy labels, resulting in sub-optimal performance.

In this paper, we reveal the impact of noisy labels on intrusion detection models from the perspective of causal associations, attributing performance degradation to local consistency of features across categories in network traffic. Motivated by this, we propose CoLD, a Collaborative Label Denoising framework for network intrusion detection. CoLD partitions the original feature set into multiple subsets and employs Local Joint Learning to disrupt local consistency, compelling the encoder to learn fine-grained and robust representations. It further applies Causal Collaborative Denoising to detect and filter noisy labels by analyzing causal divergences between multiple representations and their potentially true label, yielding a purified dataset for training a noise-resilient classifier.

Experiments on several benchmark datasets demonstrate that CoLD effectively improves classification performance and robustness to label noise, highlighting its potential for enhancing network intrusion detection systems in noisy environments.

摘要

标签噪声在网络入侵检测中提出了重大挑战，导致错误分类和检测准确性下降。现有处理噪声标签的方法通常缺乏对网络流量的深入洞察，盲目重建标签分布以过滤具有噪声标签的样本，导致次优性能。

在本文中，我们从因果关联的角度揭示噪声标签对入侵检测模型的影响，将性能下降归因于网络流量中跨类别特征的局部一致性。受此启发，我们提出了CoLD，一个用于网络入侵检测的协作标签去噪框架。CoLD将原始特征集划分为多个子集，并采用局部联合学习来破坏局部一致性，迫使编码器学习细粒度和鲁棒的表示。它进一步应用因果协作去噪，通过分析多个表示与其潜在真实标签之间的因果差异来检测和过滤噪声标签，产生用于训练抗噪声分类器的纯化数据集。

在几个基准数据集上的实验表明，CoLD有效地提高了分类性能和对标签噪声的鲁棒性，突出了其在噪声环境中增强网络入侵检测系统的潜力。

14. Entente: Federated Learning for Cross-Silo IDS

Authors: Jiacen Xu (Microsoft), Chenang Li, Yu Zheng, Zhou Li (UC Irvine)

Abstract

Graph-based Network Intrusion Detection Systems (GNIDS) have gained significant momentum in detecting sophisticated cyber-attacks, such as Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs), within and across organizational boundaries. Though achieving satisfying detection accuracy and demonstrating adaptability to ever-changing attacks and normal patterns, existing GNIDS predominantly assume a centralized data setting. However, flexible data collection is not always realistic or achievable due to increasing constraints by privacy regulations and operational limitations.

We argue that the practical development of GNIDS requires accounting for distributed collection settings and we leverage Federated Learning (FL) as a viable paradigm to address this prominent challenge. We observe that naively applying FL to GNIDS is unlikely to be effective, due to issues like graph heterogeneity over clients and the diverse design choices taken by different GNIDS. We address these issues with a set of novel techniques tailored to the graph datasets, including reference graph synthesis, graph sketching and adaptive contribution scaling, eventually developing a new system ENTENTE.

By leveraging the domain knowledge, ENTENTE can achieve effectiveness, scalability and robustness simultaneously. Empirical evaluation on the large-scale LANL, OpTC and Pivoting datasets shows that ENTENTE outperforms the SOTA FL baselines. We also evaluate ENTENTE under FL poisoning attacks tailored to the GNIDS setting, showing the robustness by bounding the attack success rate to low values. Overall, our study suggests a promising direction to build cross-silo GNIDS.

摘要

基于图的网络入侵检测系统(GNIDS)在组织边界内和跨组织边界检测复杂的网络攻击(如高级持续威胁APT)方面获得了显著的动力。虽然实现了令人满意的检测准确性并展示了对不断变化的攻击和正常模式的适应性，但现有的GNIDS主要假设集中式数据设置。然而，由于隐私法规和操作限制的日益增加的约束，灵活的数据收集并不总是现实或可实现的。

我们认为GNIDS的实际开发需要考虑分布式收集设置，我们利用联邦学习(FL)作为解决这一突出挑战的可行范式。我们观察到天真地将FL应用于GNIDS不太可能有效，因为诸如客户端之间的图异构性和不同GNIDS采取的多样化设计选择等问题。我们用一组针对图数据集定制的新颖技术解决这些问题，包括参考图合成、图草图和自适应贡献缩放，最终开发了一个新系统ENTENTE。

通过利用领域知识，ENTENTE可以同时实现有效性、可扩展性和鲁棒性。在大规模LANL、OpTC和Pivoting数据集上的经验评估表明，ENTENTE优于SOTA FL基线。我们还在针对GNIDS设置定制的FL投毒攻击下评估ENTENTE，通过将攻击成功率限制在低值来显示鲁棒性。总的来说，我们的研究为构建跨筒仓GNIDS提供了一个有前途的方向。

1. ADGFUZZ: Assignment Dependency-Guided Fuzzing for Robotic Vehicles

Authors: Yuncheng Wang, Yaowen Zheng (IIE, CAS; UCAS), Puzhuo Liu (Ant Group; Tsinghua), Dongliang Fang, Jiaxing Cheng, Dingyi Shi, Limin Sun (IIE, CAS; UCAS)

Abstract

Robotic vehicles (RVs) play an increasingly vital role in modern society, with widespread applications in both commercial and military contexts. RV control software is the core of RV systems, which maintains proper operation by continuously computing the vehicle’s internal state, sensor readings, and external inputs to adjust the system’s behavior accordingly. However, the vast combination space of configurable parameters, command inputs, and environment-sensed data in RV software introduces significant security risks to the system. Existing fuzzing techniques face substantial challenges in effectively exploring this vast input space while uncovering deep bugs.

To address these challenges, we propose ADGFuzz, a novel fuzzing framework specifically designed to detect assignment statement bugs in RV control software. ADGFuzz statically constructs an Assignment Dependency Graph (ADG) to capture inter-variable dependencies within the program. These dependencies are then propagated to the RV input space by leveraging naming similarities, resulting in a targeted set of inputs referred to as the matched input set (MIS). Building upon this, ADGFuzz performs entropy-aware fuzzing over the MISs, thereby enhancing the overall efficiency of bug discovery. In our evaluation, ADGFuzz uncovered 87 unique bugs across three RV types, 78 of which were previously unknown. All found bugs were responsibly disclosed to the developers, and 16 have been confirmed for fixing.

摘要

机器人车辆(RV)在现代社会中发挥着越来越重要的作用，在商业和军事领域都有广泛应用。RV控制软件是RV系统的核心，通过不断计算车辆的内部状态、传感器读数和外部输入来调整系统行为，从而保持正常运行。然而，RV软件中可配置参数、命令输入和环境感知数据的庞大组合空间给系统带来了重大安全风险。现有的模糊测试技术在有效探索这个庞大输入空间并发现深层错误方面面临重大挑战。

为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了ADGFuzz，这是一个专门设计用于检测RV控制软件中赋值语句错误的新型模糊测试框架。ADGFuzz静态构建赋值依赖图(ADG)以捕获程序内的变量间依赖关系。然后通过利用命名相似性将这些依赖关系传播到RV输入空间，产生称为匹配输入集(MIS)的目标输入集。在此基础上，ADGFuzz对MIS执行熵感知模糊测试，从而提高错误发现的整体效率。在我们的评估中，ADGFuzz在三种RV类型中发现了87个唯一错误，其中78个是以前未知的。所有发现的错误都已负责任地披露给开发人员，其中16个已被确认修复。

2. FirmAgent: LLM-Assisted IoT Firmware Vulnerability Discovery

Authors: Jiangan Ji (Information Engineering University, Tsinghua), Chao Zhang (Tsinghua), Shuitao Gan (LACE), Lin Jian, Hangtian Liu, Tieming Liu (Information Engineering University), Lei Zheng (Tsinghua), Zhipeng Jia (Information Engineering University)

Abstract

The rapid proliferation of IoT devices has introduced substantial security vulnerabilities. Existing vulnerability detection techniques exhibit various weaknesses: static analysis solutions (including large language models, LLMs) suffer from high false positives and provide no PoC (proof-of-concept) samples, while dynamic analysis solutions (e.g., fuzzing) often have high false negatives.

To address these challenges, we present FirmAgent, the first hybrid solution that leverages fuzzing to assist LLM agents in finding vulnerabilities in IoT firmware. Our design is motivated by the key observation that fuzzing can accurately identify input-related code points in firmware, while static analysis can thoroughly analyze program paths starting from those code points. FirmAgent utilizes fuzzing to collect runtime input points (i.e., taint sources) and reconstruct potential vulnerability paths. Then, it applies an LLM agent to perform context-aware taint analysis along the potential paths and another LLM agent to refine the fuzzing-generated testcase to generate PoC testcases.

We evaluate FirmAgent on 14 real-world IoT firmware. It identifies 182 vulnerabilities with a precision of 91%, including 140 previously unknown vulnerabilities, 17 of which have been assigned CVE numbers. Our results demonstrate that FirmAgent substantially outperforms SOTA tools in both detection capability and precision.

摘要

物联网设备的快速普及带来了大量安全漏洞。现有的漏洞检测技术表现出各种弱点：静态分析解决方案(包括大型语言模型LLM)存在高误报率且不提供PoC(概念验证)样本，而动态分析解决方案(例如模糊测试)通常具有高漏报率。

为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了FirmAgent，这是第一个利用模糊测试来协助LLM代理在物联网固件中查找漏洞的混合解决方案。我们的设计受到关键观察的启发：模糊测试可以准确识别固件中与输入相关的代码点，而静态分析可以彻底分析从这些代码点开始的程序路径。FirmAgent利用模糊测试收集运行时输入点(即污点源)并重建潜在的漏洞路径。然后，它应用LLM代理沿着潜在路径执行上下文感知的污点分析，并使用另一个LLM代理细化模糊测试生成的测试用例以生成PoC测试用例。

我们在14个真实世界的物联网固件上评估了FirmAgent。它以91%的精度识别了182个漏洞，包括140个以前未知的漏洞，其中17个已被分配CVE编号。我们的结果表明，FirmAgent在检测能力和精度方面都显著优于SOTA工具。

3. FirmCross: Detecting Taint Vulnerabilities in C-Lua Hybrid Services

Authors: Runhao Liu (NUDT), Jiarun Dai, Haoyu Xiao, Yuan Zhang (Fudan), Yeqi Mou, Lukai Xu, Bo Yu, Baosheng Wang (NUDT), Min Yang (Fudan)

Abstract

Static taint analysis has become a fundamental technique to detect vulnerabilities implied in web services of Linux-based firmware. However, existing works commonly oversimplify the composition of firmware web services. Specifically, only C binaries (i.e., those extracted from the target firmware) are considered within the scope of vulnerability detection. In this work, we observe that modern firmware extensively combines Lua scripts/bytecode and C binaries to implement hybrid web services, and obviously, those C-binary-oriented vulnerability detection techniques can hardly achieve satisfactory performance.

In light of this, we propose FirmCross, an automated taint-style vulnerability detector dedicated for C-Lua hybrid web services. Compared to existing detectors, FirmCross can automatically de-obfuscate the Lua bytecode in target firmware, additionally identify distinctive taint sources in Lua codespace, and systematically capture the C-Lua cross-language taint flow. In the evaluation, FirmCross detects 6.82X ~ 14.5X more vulnerabilities than SoTA approaches (i.e., MangoDFA and LuaTaint) in a dataset containing 73 firmware images from 11 vendors. Notably, FirmCross helps identify 610 0-day vulnerabilities among target firmware images. After reporting these vulnerabilities to vendors, till now, 31 vulnerability IDs have been assigned.

摘要

12. DualStrike: Keystroke Eavesdropping and Injection

Authors: Xiaomeng Chen, Jike Wang, Zhenyu Chen, Xinbing Wang, Dongyao Chen (Shanghai Jiao Tong University), Qi Alfred Chen (UC Irvine)

Abstract

We discover that enabling both eavesdropping and non-invasive, per-key injection is viable on keyboards, in particular, the fast-emerging commodity Hall-effect keyboards. This paper introduces DualStrike, a new attack system that allows attackers to remotely listen to victim input and control any key on a Hall-effect keyboard. This capability opens doors to severe attacks (e.g., file deletion, private key theft, and tampering) based on the victim’s input and context, all without requiring hardware or software modifications to the victim’s computer.

We present several key innovations in DualStrike, including a novel, compact electromagnet-based hardware design for high-frequency magnetic spoofing, a synchronization-free attack scheme, and a magnetometer-based listening mechanism using commercial off-the-shelf components. 1. DirtyFree: Simplified Data-Oriented Programming in the Linux Kernel

Authors: Yoochan Lee (Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy), Hyuk Kwon (Theori, Inc.), Thorsten Holz (Max Planck Institute for Security and Privacy)

Abstract

With the advent of Kernel Control-Flow Integrity (KCFI), Data-Oriented Programming (DOP) has emerged as an essential alternative to traditional control-flow hijacking techniques such as Return-Oriented Programming (ROP). Unlike control-flow attacks, DOP manipulates kernel data-flow to achieve privilege escalation without violating control-flow integrity. However, traditional DOP attacks remain complex and exhibit limited practicality due to their multistage nature, typically requiring heap address leakage, arbitrary address read, and arbitrary address write capabilities. Each stage imposes strict constraints on the selection and usage of kernel objects.

To address these limitations, we introduce DirtyFree, a systematic exploitation method that leverages the arbitrary free primitive. This primitive enables the forced deallocation of attacker-controlled kernel objects, significantly reducing exploitability requirements and simplifying the overall exploitation process. DirtyFree provides a systematic method for identifying suitable arbitrary free objects across diverse kernel caches and presents a structured exploitation strategy targeting security-critical objects such as cred. Through extensive evaluation, we successfully identified 14 arbitrary free objects covering most kernel caches, demonstrating DirtyFree’s practical effectiveness by successfully exploiting 24 real-world kernel vulnerabilities. Additionally, we propose and implement two mitigation techniques designed to mitigate DirtyFree, effectively preventing exploitation while incurring negligible performance overhead (i.e., 0.28% and -0.55%, respectively).

摘要

随着内核控制流完整性(KCFI)的出现，数据导向编程(DOP)已成为传统控制流劫持技术(如返回导向编程ROP)的重要替代方案。与控制流攻击不同，DOP操纵内核数据流以实现权限提升，而不违反控制流完整性。然而，传统的DOP攻击由于其多阶段性质而仍然复杂且实用性有限，通常需要堆地址泄漏、任意地址读取和任意地址写入能力。每个阶段对内核对象的选择和使用都施加严格的约束。

为了解决这些限制，我们引入了DirtyFree，这是一种利用任意释放原语的系统化利用方法。该原语能够强制释放攻击者控制的内核对象，显著降低可利用性要求并简化整体利用过程。DirtyFree提供了一种系统方法，用于跨不同内核缓存识别合适的任意释放对象，并提出了针对cred等安全关键对象的结构化利用策略。通过广泛评估，我们成功识别了覆盖大多数内核缓存的14个任意释放对象，通过成功利用24个真实世界内核漏洞展示了DirtyFree的实际有效性。此外，我们提出并实现了两种旨在缓解DirtyFree的缓解技术，有效防止利用，同时产生可忽略的性能开销(即0.28%和-0.55%)。

2. Cross-Cache Attacks for the Linux Kernel via PCP Massaging

Authors: Claudio Migliorelli (IBM Research Europe - Zurich), Andrea Mambretti (IBM Research Europe - Zurich), Alessandro Sorniotti (IBM Research Europe - Zurich), Vittorio Zaccaria (Politecnico di Milano), Anil Kurmus (IBM Research Europe - Zurich)

Abstract

Kernel memory allocators remain a critical attack surface, despite decades of research into memory corruption defenses. While recent mitigation strategies have diminished the effectiveness of conventional attack techniques, we show that robust cross-cache attacks are still feasible and pose a significant threat. In this paper, we introduce PCPLost, a cross-cache memory massaging technique that bypasses mainline mitigations by carefully using side channels to infer the kernel allocator’s internal state. We demonstrate that vulnerabilities such as out-of-bounds (OOB) — and, via pivoting, use-after-free (UAF) and double-free (DF) — can be exploited reliably through a cross-cache attack, across all generic caches, even in the presence of noise. We validate the generality and robustness of our approach by exploiting 6 publicly disclosed CVEs by using PCPLost, and discuss possible mitigations. The significant reliability (over 90% in most cases) of our approach in obtaining a cross-cache layout suggests that current mitigation strategies fail to offer comprehensive protection against such attacks within the Linux kernel.

摘要

尽管几十年来对内存损坏防御的研究，内核内存分配器仍然是一个关键的攻击面。虽然最近的缓解策略已经减弱了传统攻击技术的有效性，但我们表明健壮的跨缓存攻击仍然可行并构成重大威胁。在本文中，我们引入了PCPLost，这是一种跨缓存内存按摩技术，通过仔细使用侧信道推断内核分配器的内部状态来绕过主线缓解措施。我们证明了诸如越界(OOB)——以及通过旋转、释放后使用(UAF)和双重释放(DF)——等漏洞可以通过跨缓存攻击在所有通用缓存中可靠地利用，即使在存在噪声的情况下。我们通过使用PCPLost利用6个公开披露的CVE来验证我们方法的通用性和鲁棒性，并讨论可能的缓解措施。我们方法在获得跨缓存布局方面的显著可靠性(在大多数情况下超过90%)表明，当前的缓解策略未能在Linux内核内提供针对此类攻击的全面保护。

Abstract

VMware ESXi is a widely deployed enterprise-grade Type-1 hypervisor that serves as the foundation for modern cloud infrastructure. To reinforce privilege isolation, ESXi introduced a mandatory access control mechanism in VMKernel. However, due to VMKernel’s proprietary and closed-source nature, its internal access control architecture remains largely opaque and underexplored. Prior research has focused primarily on virtual device vulnerabilities and virtual machine escape, leaving the internal access control mechanisms and privilege model of VMKernel largely unexamined.

To address this gap, we conduct the first comprehensive security analysis of VMKernel’s access control mechanism. 摘要

VMware ESXi是一个广泛部署的企业级Type-1虚拟机管理程序，是现代云基础设施的基础。为了加强权限隔离，ESXi在VMKernel中引入了强制访问控制机制。然而，由于VMKernel的专有和闭源性质，其内部访问控制架构在很大程度上仍然不透明且未被充分探索。先前的研究主要集中在虚拟设备漏洞和虚拟机逃逸上，而VMKernel的内部访问控制机制和权限模型在很大程度上未经检查。

4. Eviction Notice: Reviving and Advancing Page Cache Attacks

Authors: Sudheendra Raghav Neela, Jonas Juffinger, Lukas Maar, Daniel Gruss (Graz University of Technology)

Abstract

Page cache attacks are hardware-agnostic and can have a high temporal and spatial resolution. With mitigations deployed since 2019, only Evict+Reload-style timing measurements remain, but suffer from a very low temporal resolution and a high impact on system performance due to eviction.

In this paper, we show that the problem of page cache attacks is significantly larger than anticipated. We first present a new systematic approach to page cache attacks based on four primitives: flush, reload, evict, and monitor. From these primitives, we derive five generic attack techniques on the page cache: Flush+Monitor, Flush+Reload, Flush+Flush, Evict+Monitor, and Evict+Reload. We show mechanisms for all primitives that operate on fully up-to-date Linux kernels, bypassing existing mitigations. We demonstrate the practicality of our revived page cache attacks in three scenarios, showing that we advance the state of the art by orders of magnitude in terms of spatial and temporal attack resolution: First, the channel capacity with our fastest attack (Flush+Monitor) achieves an average capacity of 37.7 kB/s in a cross-process covert channel. Second, for low-frequency attacks, we demonstrate inter-keystroke timing and event detection attacks across processes, with a spatial resolution of 4 kB and a temporal resolution of 0.8 μs, improving the state of the art by 6 orders of magnitude. Third, in a website-fingerprinting attack, we achieve an F1 score of 90.54% in a top-100 closed-world scenario. We conclude that further mitigations are necessary against the page cache side channel.

摘要

页缓存攻击是硬件无关的，可以具有高时间和空间分辨率。自2019年部署缓解措施以来，仅剩下Evict+Reload风格的时序测量，但由于驱逐而遭受非常低的时间分辨率和对系统性能的高影响。

在本文中，我们表明页缓存攻击的问题比预期的要大得多。我们首先提出了一种基于四个原语的页缓存攻击新系统方法：刷新、重新加载、驱逐和监控。从这些原语中，我们派生出五种针对页缓存的通用攻击技术：Flush+Monitor、Flush+Reload、Flush+Flush、Evict+Monitor和Evict+Reload。我们展示了所有原语在完全最新的Linux内核上运行的机制，绕过了现有的缓解措施。我们在三个场景中展示了我们恢复的页缓存攻击的实用性，表明我们在空间和时间攻击分辨率方面将最先进技术提高了几个数量级：首先，我们最快的攻击(Flush+Monitor)的信道容量在跨进程隐蔽信道中实现了平均37.7 kB/s的容量。其次，对于低频攻击，我们演示了跨进程的击键间时序和事件检测攻击，空间分辨率为4 kB，时间分辨率为0.8 μs，将最先进技术提高了6个数量级。第三，在网站指纹攻击中，我们在top-100封闭世界场景中实现了90.54%的F1分数。我们得出结论，针对页缓存侧信道需要进一步的缓解措施。

5. Breaking Isolation: A New Perspective on Hypervisor Exploitation via Cross-Domain Attacks

Authors: Gaoning Pan (Hangzhou Dianzi University), Yiming Tao, Qinying Wang (EPFL and Zhejiang University), Chunming Wu (Zhejiang University), Mingde Hu, Yizhi Ren (Hangzhou Dianzi University), Shouling Ji (Zhejiang University)

Abstract

Hypervisors are under threat by critical memory safety vulnerabilities, with pointer corruption being one of the most prevalent and severe forms. Existing exploitation frameworks depend on identifying highly-constrained structures in the host machine and accurately determining their runtime addresses, which is ineffective in hypervisor environments where such structures are rare and further obfuscated by Address Space Layout Randomization (ASLR). We instead observe that modern virtualization environments exhibit weak memory isolation — guest memory is fully attacker-controlled yet accessible from the host, providing a reliable primitive for exploitation. Based on this observation, we present the first systematic characterization and taxonomy of Cross-Domain Attacks (CDA), a class of exploitation techniques that enable capability escalation through guest memory reuse. To automate this process, we develop a system that identifies cross-domain gadgets, matches them with corrupted pointers, synthesizes triggering inputs, and assembles complete exploit chains. Our evaluation on 15 real-world vulnerabilities across QEMU and VirtualBox shows that CDA is widely applicable and effective.

摘要

虚拟机管理程序受到关键内存安全漏洞的威胁，指针损坏是最普遍和最严重的形式之一。现有的利用框架依赖于识别主机中高度受限的结构并准确确定其运行时地址，这在虚拟机管理程序环境中是无效的，因为此类结构很少见，并且被地址空间布局随机化(ASLR)进一步混淆。相反，我们观察到现代虚拟化环境表现出弱内存隔离——客户内存完全由攻击者控制但可从主机访问，为利用提供了可靠的原语。基于这一观察，我们提出了**跨域攻击(CDA)**的首次系统特征化和分类，这是一类通过客户内存重用实现能力升级的利用技术。为了自动化这个过程，我们开发了一个系统，该系统识别跨域小工具，将它们与损坏的指针匹配，合成触发输入，并组装完整的利用链。我们对QEMU和VirtualBox中15个真实世界漏洞的评估表明，CDA具有广泛的适用性和有效性。

6. Bit of a Close Talker: A Practical Guide to Serverless Cloud Co-Location Attacks

Abstract

Serverless computing has revolutionized cloud computing by offering users an efficient, cost-effective way to develop and deploy applications without managing infrastructure details. However, serverless cloud users remain vulnerable to various types of attacks, including micro-architectural side-channel attacks. These attacks typically rely on the physical co-location of victim and attacker instances, and attackers need to exploit cloud schedulers to achieve co-location with victims. Therefore, it is crucial to study vulnerabilities in serverless cloud schedulers and assess the security of different serverless scheduling algorithms. Authors: Hongyue Jin (Clemson University), Yanan Guo (University of Rochester), Zhenkai Zhang (Clemson University)

Abstract

With the growing adoption of virtualized GPUs in cloud computing, the potential security implications associated with GPU sharing among multiple tenants have largely been overlooked. This paper takes a foundational step in revealing these risks by investigating information leakage through GPU microarchitectural components. Specifically, we develop a Prime+Probe attack primitive tailored to the translation lookaside buffers (TLBs) in virtualized NVIDIA GPUs. We discuss several unique challenges posed by the GPU virtualization environment and demonstrate how our design effectively overcomes them. Leveraging this primitive, we conduct two cross-VM side-channel attack case studies in a cloud setting: a cheating exploit in the game Counter-Strike 2 that reveals hidden opponents and a website fingerprinting attack that identifies web pages browsed by users of virtual desktops. To the best of our knowledge, these are the first side-channel attacks demonstrated against virtualized GPUs in cloud settings, highlighting previously unknown security risks that warrant further investigation.

摘要

随着虚拟化GPU在云计算中的日益采用，与多个租户之间GPU共享相关的潜在安全影响在很大程度上被忽视。本文通过调查通过GPU微架构组件的信息泄漏来揭示这些风险，迈出了基础性的一步。具体而言，我们开发了一个针对虚拟化NVIDIA GPU中的转换后备缓冲器(TLB)定制的Prime+Probe攻击原语。我们讨论了GPU虚拟化环境带来的几个独特挑战，并演示了我们的设计如何有效克服它们。利用此原语，我们在云设置中进行了两个跨VM侧信道攻击案例研究：在游戏Counter-Strike 2中揭示隐藏对手的作弊利用，以及识别虚拟桌面用户浏览的网页的网站指纹攻击。据我们所知，这些是针对云设置中虚拟化GPU演示的首次侧信道攻击，突出了值得进一步调查的先前未知的安全风险。

15. Automated Code Annotation with LLMs for Establishing TEE Boundaries

Authors: Varun Gadey, Melanie Goetz, Christoph Sendner (University of Würzburg), Sampo Sovio (Huawei Technologies), Alexandra Dmitrienko (University of Wuerzburg)

Abstract

Modern systems increasingly rely on Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), such as Intel SGX and ARM TrustZone, to securely isolate sensitive code and reduce the Trusted Computing Base (TCB).

However, identifying the precise regions of code especially those involving cryptographic logic that should reside within a TEE remains challenging, as it requires deep manual inspection and is not supported by automated tools yet. To solve this open problem, we propose LLM based Code Annotation Logic (LLM-CAL), a tool that automates the identification of security-sensitive code regions at scale by leveraging most recent and advanced Large Language Models (LLMs). Our approach leverages foundational LLMs (Gemma-2B, CodeGemma-2B, and LLaMA-7B), which we fine-tuned using a newly collected and manually labeled dataset of over 4,000 C source files. We encode local context features, global semantic information, and structural metadata into compact input sequences that guide the model in capturing subtle patterns of security sensitivity in code. The fine-tuning process is based on quantized LoRA—a parameter-efficient technique that introduces lightweight, trainable adapters into the LLM architecture. To support practical deployment, we developed a scalable pipeline for data preprocessing and inference. LLM-CAL achieves an F1 score of 98.40% and a recall of 97.50% in identifying sensitive and non-sensitive code. It represents the first effort to automate the annotation of cryptographic security-sensitive code for TEE-enabled platforms, aiming to minimize the Trusted Computing Base (TCB) and optimize TEE usage to enhance overall system security.

摘要

现代系统越来越依赖可信执行环境(TEE)，如Intel SGX和ARM TrustZone，以安全隔离敏感代码并减少可信计算基础(TCB)。

然而，识别应该驻留在TEE中的精确代码区域，特别是那些涉及密码逻辑的代码区域，仍然具有挑战性，因为这需要深入的手动检查，并且尚未得到自动化工具的支持。为了解决这个开放问题，我们提出了基于LLM的代码注释逻辑(LLM-CAL)，这是一个通过利用最新和最先进的大型语言模型(LLM)大规模自动化识别安全敏感代码区域的工具。我们的方法利用基础LLM(Gemma-2B、CodeGemma-2B和LLaMA-7B)，我们使用新收集和手动标记的超过4,000个C源文件的数据集对其进行了微调。我们将本地上下文特征、全局语义信息和结构元数据编码为紧凑的输入序列，以指导模型捕获代码中安全敏感性的微妙模式。微调过程基于量化LoRA——一种参数高效技术，将轻量级、可训练的适配器引入LLM架构。为了支持实际部署，我们开发了一个用于数据预处理和推理的可扩展管道。LLM-CAL在识别敏感和非敏感代码方面实现了98.40%的F1分数和97.50%的召回率。它代表了自动化注释TEE启用平台的密码安全敏感代码的首次努力，旨在最小化可信计算基础(TCB)并优化TEE使用以增强整体系统安全性。

1. A Causal Perspective for Enhancing Jailbreak Attack and Defense

Authors: Licheng Pan (Zhejiang University), Yunsheng Lu (University of Chicago), Jiexi Liu (Alibaba Group), Jialing Tao (Alibaba Group), Haozhe Feng (Zhejiang University), Hui Xue (Alibaba Group), Zhixuan Chu (Zhejiang University), Kui Ren (Zhejiang University)

Abstract

Uncovering the mechanisms behind “jailbreaks” in large language models (LLMs) is crucial for enhancing their safety and reliability, yet these mechanisms remain poorly understood. Existing studies predominantly analyze jailbreak prompts by probing latent representations, often overlooking the causal relationships between interpretable prompt features and jailbreak occurrences. In this work, we propose Causal Analyst, a framework that integrates LLMs into data-driven causal discovery to identify the direct causes of jailbreaks and leverage them for both attack and defense. We introduce a comprehensive dataset comprising 35k jailbreak attempts across seven LLMs, systematically constructed from 100 attack templates and 50 harmful queries, annotated with 37 meticulously designed human-readable prompt features.

By jointly training LLM-based prompt encoding and GNN-based causal graph learning, we reconstruct causal pathways linking prompt features to jailbreak responses. Our analysis reveals that specific features, such as “Positive Character” and “Number of Task Steps”, act as direct causal drivers of jailbreaks. We demonstrate the practical utility of these insights through two applications: (1) a Jailbreaking Enhancer that targets identified causal features to significantly boost attack success rates on public benchmarks, and (2) a Guardrail Advisor that utilizes the learned causal graph to extract true malicious intent from obfuscated queries.

Extensive experiments, including baseline comparisons and causal structure validation, confirm the robustness of our causal analysis and its superiority over non-causal approaches. Our results suggest that analyzing jailbreak features from a causal perspective is an effective and interpretable approach for improving LLM reliability.

摘要

揭示大型语言模型(LLM)中”越狱”背后的机制对于增强其安全性和可靠性至关重要，但这些机制仍然知之甚少。现有研究主要通过探测潜在表示来分析越狱提示，往往忽略了可解释提示特征与越狱发生之间的因果关系。在这项工作中，我们提出了**因果分析师(Causal Analyst)**框架，它将LLM集成到数据驱动的因果发现中，以识别越狱的直接原因并将其用于攻击和防御。我们引入了一个包含35k次越狱尝试的综合数据集，涵盖七个LLM，系统地从100个攻击模板和50个有害查询构建，并用37个精心设计的人类可读提示特征进行标注。

通过联合训练基于LLM的提示编码和基于GNN的因果图学习，我们重建了将提示特征与越狱响应联系起来的因果路径。我们的分析揭示了特定特征(如”积极角色”和”任务步骤数”)是越狱的直接因果驱动因素。我们通过两个应用演示了这些见解的实用性：(1)越狱增强器针对已识别的因果特征显著提高公共基准测试的攻击成功率；(2)防护顾问利用学习到的因果图从混淆查询中提取真实恶意意图。

包括基线比较和因果结构验证在内的广泛实验证实了我们因果分析的鲁棒性及其优于非因果方法的优势。我们的结果表明，从因果角度分析越狱特征是提高LLM可靠性的有效且可解释的方法。

2. ACE: A Security Architecture for LLM-Integrated App Systems

Authors: Evan Li, Tushin Mallick, Evan Rose, William Robertson, Alina Oprea, Cristina Nita-Rotaru (Northeastern University)

Abstract

LLM-integrated app systems extend the utility of Large Language Models (LLMs) with third-party apps that are invoked by a system LLM using interleaved planning and execution phases to answer user queries. These systems introduce new attack vectors where malicious apps can cause integrity violation of planning or execution, availability breakdown, or privacy compromise during execution.

In this work, we identify new attacks impacting the integrity of planning, as well as the integrity and availability of execution in LLM-integrated apps, and demonstrate them against IsolateGPT, a recent solution designed to mitigate attacks from malicious apps. We propose Abstract-Concrete-Execute (ACE), a new secure architecture for LLM-integrated app systems that provides security guarantees for system planning and execution. Specifically, ACE decouples planning into two phases by first creating an abstract execution plan using only trusted information, and then mapping the abstract plan to a concrete plan using installed system apps. We verify that the plans generated by our system satisfy user-specified secure information flow constraints via static analysis on the structured plan output. During execution, ACE enforces data and capability barriers between apps, and ensures that the execution is conducted according to the trusted abstract plan. We show experimentally that ACE is secure against attacks from the InjecAgent and Agent Security Bench benchmarks for indirect prompt injection, and our newly introduced attacks. We also evaluate the utility of ACE in realistic environments, using the Tool Usage suite from the LangChain benchmark. Our architecture represents a significant advancement towards hardening LLM-based systems using system security principles.

摘要

LLM集成应用系统通过第三方应用扩展了大型语言模型(LLM)的实用性，系统LLM使用交织的计划和执行阶段调用这些应用来回答用户查询。这些系统引入了新的攻击向量，恶意应用可能导致计划或执行的完整性违规、可用性崩溃或执行期间的隐私泄露。

在这项工作中，我们识别了影响LLM集成应用中计划完整性以及执行完整性和可用性的新攻击，并针对IsolateGPT(一个旨在缓解恶意应用攻击的最新解决方案)进行了演示。我们提出了**抽象-具体-执行(ACE)**架构，这是一个为LLM集成应用系统提供计划和执行安全保证的新型安全架构。具体而言，ACE将计划解耦为两个阶段：首先仅使用可信信息创建抽象执行计划，然后使用已安装的系统应用将抽象计划映射到具体计划。我们通过对结构化计划输出的静态分析验证系统生成的计划满足用户指定的安全信息流约束。在执行期间，ACE在应用之间实施数据和能力屏障，并确保执行按照可信的抽象计划进行。我们通过实验表明，ACE能够抵御InjecAgent和Agent Security Bench基准测试中的间接提示注入攻击以及我们新引入的攻击。我们还使用LangChain基准测试的工具使用套件评估了ACE在现实环境中的实用性。我们的架构代表了使用系统安全原则加固基于LLM系统的重大进展。

3. Attention is All You Need to Defend Against Indirect Prompt Injection Attacks in LLMs

Authors: Yinan Zhong, Qianhao Miao, Yanjiao Chen, Jiangyi Deng, Yushi Cheng, Wenyuan Xu (Zhejiang University)

Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) have been integrated into many applications (e.g., web agents) to perform more sophisticated tasks. However, LLM-empowered applications are vulnerable to Indirect Prompt Injection (IPI) attacks, where instructions are injected via untrustworthy external data sources. This paper presents Rennervate, a defense framework to detect and prevent IPI attacks. Rennervate leverages attention features to detect the covert injection at a fine-grained token level, enabling precise sanitization that neutralizes IPI attacks while maintaining LLM functionalities. Specifically, the token-level detector is materialized with a 2-step attentive pooling mechanism, which aggregates attention heads and response tokens for IPI detection and sanitization. Moreover, we establish a fine-grained IPI dataset, FIPI, to be open-sourced to support further research. Extensive experiments verify that Rennervate outperforms 15 commercial and academic IPI defense methods, achieving high precision on 5 LLMs and 6 datasets. We also demonstrate that Rennervate is transferable to unseen attacks and robust against adaptive adversaries.

摘要

大型语言模型(LLM)已被集成到许多应用程序(例如Web代理)中以执行更复杂的任务。然而，LLM赋能的应用程序容易受到间接提示注入(IPI)攻击，其中指令通过不可信的外部数据源注入。本文提出了Rennervate防御框架来检测和防止IPI攻击。Rennervate利用注意力特征在细粒度token级别检测隐蔽注入，实现精确清理以中和IPI攻击同时保持LLM功能。具体而言，token级检测器通过两步注意力池化机制实现，该机制聚合注意力头和响应token进行IPI检测和清理。此外，我们建立了细粒度IPI数据集FIPI，将开源以支持进一步研究。广泛的实验验证了Rennervate优于15种商业和学术IPI防御方法，在5个LLM和6个数据集上实现了高精度。我们还证明了Rennervate对未见攻击具有可迁移性，对自适应对手具有鲁棒性。

4. Beyond Jailbreak: Unveiling Risks from Blurred Capability Boundaries

Authors: Yunyi Zhang, Shibo Cui, Baojun Liu, Jingkai Yu (Tsinghua University), Min Zhang, Fan Shi (NUDT), Han Zheng (TrustAl)

Abstract

LLM applications leverage the powerful capabilities of LLMs to provide users with customized services, revolutionizing traditional application development. While the increasing prevalence of LLM-powered applications provides users with unprecedented convenience, it also brings forth new security challenges. For such an emerging ecosystem, the security community lacks sufficient understanding of the LLM application ecosystem, especially regarding the capability boundaries of the applications themselves.

In this paper, we systematically analyzed the new development paradigm and defined the concept of the LLM app capability space. We also uncovered potential new risks beyond jailbreak that arise from ambiguous capability boundaries in real-world scenarios, namely, capability downgrade and upgrade. To evaluate the impact of these risks, we designed and implemented an LLM app capability evaluation framework, LLMApp-Eval. First, we collected application metadata across 4 platforms and conducted a cross-platform ecosystem analysis. Then, we evaluated the risks for 199 popular applications among 4 platforms and 6 open-source LLMs. We identified that 178 (89.45%) potentially affected applications, which can perform tasks from more than 15 scenarios or be malicious. We even found 17 applications in our study that executed malicious tasks directly, without applying any adversarial rewriting. Furthermore, our experiments also reveal a positive correlation between the quality of prompt design and application robustness. We found that well-designed prompts enhance security, while poorly designed ones can facilitate abuse. We hope our work inspires the community to focus on the real-world risks of LLM applications and foster the development of a more robust LLM application ecosystem.

摘要

LLM应用程序利用LLM的强大功能为用户提供定制服务，彻底改变了传统的应用程序开发。虽然LLM驱动应用程序的日益普及为用户提供了前所未有的便利，但它也带来了新的安全挑战。对于这样一个新兴的生态系统，安全社区对LLM应用程序生态系统缺乏足够的了解，特别是关于应用程序本身的能力边界。

在本文中，我们系统地分析了新的开发范式并定义了LLM应用能力空间的概念。我们还揭示了在现实场景中由于模糊的能力边界而产生的超越越狱的潜在新风险，即能力降级和升级。为了评估这些风险的影响，我们设计并实现了LLM应用能力评估框架LLMApp-Eval。首先，我们收集了4个平台的应用程序元数据并进行了跨平台生态系统分析。然后，我们评估了4个平台和6个开源LLM中199个流行应用程序的风险。我们发现178个(89.45%)潜在受影响的应用程序可以执行来自15个以上场景的任务或具有恶意性。我们甚至在研究中发现17个应用程序直接执行恶意任务，而无需应用任何对抗性重写。此外，我们的实验还揭示了提示设计质量与应用程序鲁棒性之间的正相关关系。我们发现精心设计的提示增强了安全性，而设计不良的提示可能促进滥用。我们希望我们的工作能激发社区关注LLM应用程序的真实世界风险，并促进更强大的LLM应用程序生态系统的发展。

5. Bleeding Pathways: Vanishing Discriminability in LLM Hidden States Fuels Jailbreak Attacks

Authors: Yingjie Zhang, Tong Liu (IIE, CAS), Zhe Zhao (Ant Group), Guozhu Meng, Kai Chen (IIE, CAS)

Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) remain vulnerable to jailbreak attacks that exploit adversarial prompts to circumvent safety measures. Current safety fine-tuning approaches face two critical limitations. First, they often fail to strike a balance between security and utility, where stronger safety measures tend to over-reject harmless user requests. Second, they frequently miss malicious intent concealed within seemingly benign tasks, leaving models exposed to exploitation. Our work identifies a fundamental cause of these issues: during response generation, an LLM’s capacity to differentiate harmful from safe outputs deteriorates. Experimental evidence confirms this, revealing that the separability between hidden states for safe and harmful responses diminishes as generation progresses. This weakening discrimination forces models to make compliance judgments earlier in the generation process, restricting their ability to recognize developing harmful intent and contributing to both aforementioned failures.

To mitigate this vulnerability, we introduce DEEPALIGN - an inherent defense framework that enhances the safety of LLMs. By applying contrastive hidden-state steering at the midpoint of response generation, DEEPALIGN amplifies the separation between harmful and benign hidden states, enabling continuous intrinsic toxicity detection and intervention throughout the generation process. Moreover, it facilitates contextually appropriate safe responses to harmful queries, thereby expanding the feasible space of safe responses. Evaluations demonstrate DEEPALIGN’s efficacy. Across diverse LLMs spanning varying architectures and scales, it reduced attack success rates of nine distinct jailbreak attacks to near-zero or minimal. Crucially, it preserved model capability while reducing over-refusal. Models equipped with DEEPALIGN exhibited up to 3.5% lower error rates in rejecting challenging benign queries and maintained standard task performance with less than 1% decline.

摘要

大型语言模型(LLM)仍然容易受到利用对抗性提示规避安全措施的越狱攻击。当前的安全微调方法面临两个关键限制。首先，它们往往无法在安全性和实用性之间取得平衡，更强的安全措施往往会过度拒绝无害的用户请求。其次，它们经常遗漏隐藏在看似良性任务中的恶意意图，使模型暴露于利用之下。我们的工作确定了这些问题的根本原因：在响应生成期间，LLM区分有害和安全输出的能力会恶化。实验证据证实了这一点，揭示了随着生成的进行，安全和有害响应的隐藏状态之间的可分离性会减弱。这种减弱的辨别力迫使模型在生成过程的早期做出合规性判断，限制了它们识别发展中的有害意图的能力，并导致上述两个失败。

为了缓解这一漏洞，我们引入了DEEPALIGN——一个增强LLM安全性的固有防御框架。通过在响应生成的中点应用对比隐藏状态引导，DEEPALIGN放大了有害和良性隐藏状态之间的分离，在整个生成过程中实现持续的内在毒性检测和干预。此外，它促进了对有害查询的上下文适当的安全响应，从而扩展了安全响应的可行空间。评估证明了DEEPALIGN的有效性。在跨越不同架构和规模的各种LLM中，它将九种不同越狱攻击的攻击成功率降低到接近零或最小。至关重要的是，它在减少过度拒绝的同时保留了模型能力。配备DEEPALIGN的模型在拒绝具有挑战性的良性查询时表现出高达3.5%的较低错误率，并在标准任务性能上保持不到1%的下降。

6. Cache Me, Catch You: Cache Related Security Threats in LLM Serving Frameworks

Authors: XiangFan Wu (Ocean University of China; QI-ANXIN), Lingyun Ying, Guoqiang Chen (QI-ANXIN), Yacong Gu (Tsinghua), Haipeng Qu (Ocean University of China)

Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) are rapidly reshaping digital interactions. Their performance and efficiency are critically dependent on advanced caching mechanisms, such as prefix caching and semantic caching. However, these mechanisms introduce a new attack surface. Unlike prior work focused on LLMs poisoning attacks during the training phase, this paper presents the first comprehensive investigation into cache-related security risks that arise during the LLM inference-time.

We conducted a systematic study of the cache implementations in mainstream LLM serving frameworks and then identified six novel attack vectors categorized as: (1) User-oriented Fraud Attacks, which manipulate cache entries to deliver malicious content to users via prefix cache collisions and semantic fuzzy poisoning; and (2) System Integrity Attacks, which exploit cache vulnerabilities to bypass security checks, such as using block-wise or multimodal collisions to evade content moderation.

Our experiments on leading open-source frameworks validated these attack vectors and evaluated their impact and cost. Furthermore, we proposed five multilayer defense strategies and assessed their effectiveness. We responsibly disclosed our findings to affected vendors, including vLLM, SGLang, GPTCache, AIBrix, rtp-llm and LMDeploy. All of them have acknowledged the vulnerabilities, and notably, vLLM, GPTCache, and AIBrix have adopted our proposed mitigation methods and fixed their vulnerabilities.

恶意软件对关键计算基础设施构成越来越大的威胁，推动了对更高级检测和分析方法的需求。虽然原始二进制恶意软件分类器显示出希望，但它们在功能上受到限制，并且在建模长序列的挑战上苦苦挣扎。与此同时，自然语言处理中大型语言模型(LLM)的兴起展示了在异构数据集上训练的大规模自监督模型的力量，为众多下游任务提供了灵活的表示。这些模型背后的成功植根于其训练数据的规模和质量、其神经架构的表现力和可扩展性，以及它们以自监督方式从未标记数据中学习的能力。

在这项工作中，我们朝着开发大型恶意软件语言模型(LMLM)(LLM的恶意软件类比)迈出了第一步。我们解决了这一目标的核心方面，即关于数据、模型、预训练和微调的问题。通过使用语言建模目标预训练恶意软件分类模型，我们能够将不同实际恶意软件分类任务的下游性能平均提高1.1%，最高提高28.6%，表明这些模型可能会成功取代原始二进制恶意软件分类器。

15. Cascading and Proxy Membership Inference Attacks

Authors: Yuntao Du, Jiacheng Li, Yuetian Chen, Kaiyuan Zhang, Zhizhen Yuan, Hanshen Xiao, Bruno Ribeiro, Ninghui Li (Purdue University)

Abstract

A Membership Inference Attack (MIA) assesses how much a trained machine learning model reveals about its training data by determining whether specific query instances were included in the dataset. We classify existing MIAs into adaptive or non-adaptive, depending on whether the adversary is allowed to train shadow models on membership queries. In the adaptive setting, where the adversary can train shadow models after accessing query instances, we highlight the importance of exploiting membership dependencies between instances and propose an attack-agnostic framework called Cascading Membership Inference Attack (CMIA), which incorporates membership dependencies via conditional shadow training to boost membership inference performance.

In the non-adaptive setting, where the adversary is restricted to training shadow models before obtaining membership queries, we introduce Proxy Membership Inference Attack (PMIA). PMIA employs a proxy selection strategy that identifies samples with similar behaviors to the query instance and uses their behaviors in shadow models to perform a membership posterior odds test for membership inference. We provide theoretical analyses for both attacks, and extensive experimental results demonstrate that CMIA and PMIA substantially outperform existing MIAs in both settings, particularly in the low false-positive regime, which is crucial for evaluating privacy risks.

摘要

成员推理攻击(MIA)通过确定特定查询实例是否包含在数据集中来评估训练的机器学习模型对其训练数据透露了多少信息。我们将现有的MIA分类为自适应或非自适应，具体取决于对手是否被允许在成员查询上训练影子模型。在自适应设置中，对手可以在访问查询实例后训练影子模型，我们强调了利用实例之间的成员依赖关系的重要性，并提出了一个攻击无关的框架，称为级联成员推理攻击(CMIA)，它通过条件影子训练整合成员依赖关系以提高成员推理性能。

在非自适应设置中，对手被限制在获得成员查询之前训练影子模型，我们引入了代理成员推理攻击(PMIA)。PMIA采用代理选择策略，识别与查询实例具有相似行为的样本，并使用它们在影子模型中的行为执行成员后验赔率测试以进行成员推理。我们为两种攻击提供了理论分析，广泛的实验结果表明，CMIA和PMIA在两种设置中都大大优于现有的MIA，特别是在低假阳性区域，这对于评估隐私风险至关重要。

1. Anansi: Scalable Job Scam Detection System

Paper: “Scalable Characterization of Message-Based Job Scams”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24223

Key Findings

Researchers have developed Anansi, the first scalable end-to-end measurement pipeline designed to systematically engage with and analyze job-based smishing scams. This automated system combines:

  • Large Language Models (LLMs) for natural conversation with scammers
  • Automated browser agents to interact with scam infrastructure
  • Infrastructure fingerprinting tools for tracking campaigns

Impact

The system successfully:

  • Collected over 29,000 scam messages
  • Interacted with more than 1,900 scammers
  • Identified millions of dollars in cryptocurrency losses
  • Uncovered extensive reuse of message templates, domains, and crypto wallets

Significance

This research demonstrates the feasibility of automating scammer engagement and infrastructure analysis, offering a new methodological foundation for studying large-scale fraud ecosystems. The system revealed sophisticated deceptive techniques including domain fronting and brand impersonation.

2. SAILOR: Ultra-Lightweight RISC-V for IoT Security

Paper: “A Scalable and Energy-Efficient Ultra-Lightweight RISC-V for IoT Security”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24166

Innovation

SAILOR introduces a revolutionary modular RISC-V core family specifically designed for cryptographic applications in IoT devices. The design spans multiple data-path widths (1-, 2-, 4-, 8-, 16-, and 32-bit) to optimize for different use cases.

Performance Achievements

  • 13x improvement in performance and energy efficiency over state-of-the-art solutions
  • 59% reduction in area (hardware footprint)
  • Minimal overhead for integrating RISC-V cryptography extensions

Why It Matters

Most existing RISC-V cores for IoT prioritize either area or energy efficiency, often compromising security. SAILOR demonstrates that lightweight cryptographic features can be integrated without prohibitive overhead, making secure IoT devices more practical and affordable.

3. Jailbreak Foundry: Reproducible LLM Security Testing

Paper: “Jailbreak Foundry: From Papers to Runnable Attacks for Reproducible Benchmarking”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.24009

The Problem

Jailbreak techniques for large language models evolve faster than benchmarks, making robustness estimates quickly outdated. Different papers use different datasets and judging protocols, making comparisons difficult.

The Solution

JAILBREAK FOUNDRY (JBF) uses a multi-agent workflow to automatically translate jailbreak research papers into executable attack modules. The system features:

  • JBF-LIB: Shared contracts and reusable utilities
  • JBF-FORGE: Multi-agent paper-to-module translation
  • JBF-EVAL: Standardized evaluation framework

Results

Across 30 reproduced attacks, JBF achieved:

  • High fidelity with mean deviation of only +0.26 percentage points from reported results
  • 82.5% mean reused-code ratio, reducing implementation effort
  • 50% reduction in attack-specific code compared to original repositories

This system enables standardized evaluation of attacks across 10 victim models, creating “living benchmarks” that keep pace with the rapidly shifting security landscape.

4. Feature Transferability in IoT Botnet Detection

Paper: “Exploring Robust Intrusion Detection: A Benchmark Study of Feature Transferability in IoT Botnet Attack Detection”
Published: February 27, 2026
arXiv: 2602.23874
Conference: Accepted for ICISSP 2026

Research Focus

This comprehensive study evaluates how well intrusion detection features transfer across different IoT network environments—a critical challenge as network characteristics vary significantly across domains.

  • CICFlowMeter

Key Findings

  • Feature choice and classification algorithm critically impact transferability

Practical Impact

The paper provides actionable guidelines for:

  • Algorithm selection for cross-domain resilience
  • Designing effective intrusion detection that works across diverse IoT environments

IH&MMSec 由原先的 IH 和 ACM MM&Sec 两个 Workshops 合并而来。

For over a decade, two workshops have been shaping the landscape of research in multimedia security. On one hand, Information Hiding (aka. IH) was created in 1996 and focused on digital watermarking, steganography and steganalysis, anonymity and privacy, hard to intercept communications and covert/subliminal channels. On the other hand, the ACM Workshop on Multimedia and Security (aka. ACM MM&Sec) was initiated in 1998 and focused on data hiding, robust/perceptual hash, biometrics, video surveillance, and multimedia forensics. Key seminal works have been published in these two workshops and papers accepted for publication there attracted over 9,000 citations in total. Year after year, the two communities grew closer and the overlap between their respective scope got bigger. As a result, after 14 successful editions each, IH and ACM MMSec decided to merge in a single event in an attempt to establish synergies between the two communities while building on the reputation obtained over the years.
